Back in 2003, she married Iranian film director and producer, Amin Mahdavi. The marriage lasted 7 years and in 2011, stating "irreconcilable differences" the pair got divorced.

 

Golshiteh and first husband, Amin Mahdavi.

In 2012, a controversy stroke the starlet that made her leave her home country Iran.

She appeared in a short French black and white video with 30 other actors to promote the Caesars, (French Oscars), in which she was nominated for her character in the famous comedy French film, Si Tu Meurs, Je Te Tue (If You Die, I'll Kill You).

For the promo, the actors had to take off a part of their clothes to commit their body and soul to their art. Farahani bared her right breast in the video and stated,

"I will put flesh to your dreams."

She also posed nude in the French Madame Figaro and when the video was released, Golshifteh faced her worst nightmare. The Iranian government made announcements that Farahani was banished from the country stating,

"Iran does not need actors or artists like you. You may offer your artistic services somewhere else"

She got banned with much criticism and harsh judgments based on her character and morality. She left Iran and moved to France after that. There she met her second husband, handsome French actor, Louis Garrel and married in 2013.

The pair stayed together for a year and later got divorced in 2014, without revealing any plausible reasons.

Quick facts about Golshifteh Farahani

  • Born and raised in Tehran, Iran, Golshifteh Farahani is the dazzling Iranian actress, singer, and musician, currently living in France.
  • She is the daughter of the legendary theater actor, Behzad Farahani, and mother Fahimeh Rahim Nia. Her sister, Shaghayegh Farahani is also an Iranian actress.
  • As she was raised in a family with acting and music background, she started learning music (piano) and acting at the early age of five and later entered music school in Tehran.
  • She landed her first debut as the lead in Iranian movie The Pear Tree (1997) at the age of 14 and got Best Actress Award in 16th Fajr International Film Festival.
  • She has done tons of Iranian movie including, Haft Parde (Seven Acts), Zamaneh (Times), Jayee Digar (Somewhere Else), Deux Fereshte (Two Angels), Ashk-e-Sarma (The Tear of the Cold), Shirin and so on.
  • She did her Hollywood debut in 2008 with movie Body of Lies where she portrayed the role of Aisha.
  • The film was a major hit and she got many film projects in Hollywood after that. She has worked in  Just Like a Woman, Exodus: Gods and Kings, Eden, Go Home, My Sweet Pepperland, and There Be Dragons.
  • Her recent movie project includes Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales where she portrays the role of a sea-witch and ally of Captain Barbossa.
  • She has been married thrice and her current husband is a former surfer and meditation practitioner and psychotherapist, Christos Dorje Walker.
  • She can speak three different languages, English, French and Persian with a slight mix of Arabic and Kurdish.
  • She was the member of the international jury at the 63rd Locarno Film Festival in 2010.
  • Her net worth is estimated to be around $1 Million.
