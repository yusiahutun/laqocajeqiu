Just because it’s freezing outside doesn’t mean you have to ditch your dresses and skirts. A-line designs and pleated plaid minis will still work for winter weather as long as you’re wearing a quality pair of fleece-lined tights. These thicker alternatives are fuzzy on the inside, meaning they’ll block out the chill as well as a pair of pants. Plus, you can easily shop for fleece-lined tights on Amazon, and to save you some trouble, we’re rounding up our favorite, top-rated pairs.

Some of these fleece-lined tights can fake that sheer, translucent look, while others are footless and can be worn as leggings. Fleece-lined tights are also perfect for layering, so if your go-to pair of jeans feels a little too light for chilly temperatures, just slip these babies on underneath for an extra dose of warmth.

  • CHRLEISURE Women's Winter Warm Fleece Lined Tights

    Colors Available: 16

    Size Range: XS/S – XL/XXL

    Despite having over 14,000 reviews on Amazon, CHRLEISURE’s fleece-lined tights still have a near-5-star rating. That’s likely because there are so many different options — various colorways, footless pairs, and packs of multiples. Like most tights, they’re snug in the waist to provide a bit of support but are still stretchy enough that you won’t feel uncomfortable after you pull them on.

    The main complaint with these tights is that they tend to run a little long and that the fleece ends at the ankle, even if you buy the footed pair. However, the latter might be considered a perk for some, since that means they won’t be too thick to wear with your regular boots and shoes.
