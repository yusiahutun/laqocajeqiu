League of Legends Winterblessed 2023 Skins Leaked

 Posted on September 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

As winter is right around the corner, the upcoming Winterblessed 2023 skins have been leaked.

Since the release of League of Legends, Riot has released skins based on various events and themes. One of the most popular events is the winter holidays. During that holiday, Riot releases some winter-themed skins. Not only that, back in the early seasons, they also used to change Summoner’s Rift to a more festive winter version. But nowadays, they don’t change the map anymore.

In 2009, we got some of the first winter-themed skins in League of Legends. They are Snowbunny Nidalee, Workshop Nunu, Happy Elf Teemo, Earnest Elf Tristana, and Old Saint Zilean. And since then and till 2019, with the release of Sugar Rush skins, we have gotten winter-themed skins every year. However, in 2020 and 2021, we did not get any holiday-themed skins.

However, we got an entirely new winter skin line after a while last year. And that skin line is called Winterblessed. This skin line included skins like Winterblessed Diana, Winterblessed Warwick, Winterblessed Swain, etc. Moreover, Winterblessed Diana is her best skin.

Since the winter holidays are on the horizon, it is expected that Riot will make new holiday-themed skins. Recently, League of Legends leaker Big Bad Bear leaked that Winterblessed would return this year.

Winterblessed 2023 Skins Leaked

Here are all the leaked Winterblessed skins for now,

  • Winterblessed Sylas
  • Winterblessed Thresh
  • Winterblessed Hwei
  • Winterblessed Senna
  • Winterblessed Lucian
  • Winterblessed Camille (Prestige)

From this list, Camille is expected to receive a prestige skin. But we still do not know which skin will be the legendary. Moreover, Winterblessed will also be League’s upcoming champion Hwei‘s launch skin.

Release Date

The Winterblessed skins are expected to launch on Patch 13.24, scheduled for Wednesday, December 6, 2023.
