Holly Willoughby is left red-faced as she giggles over a 'girthy' banana

 Posted on February 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  597 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Holly Willoughby was left red-faced as she giggled over a banana that had 'some girth' during Wednesday's This Morning.

The TV presenter, 39, and her co-star Phillip Schofield were in hysterics as they had a look at an accidentally racy snap as it featured two bananas in one piece of fruit.

Discussing the picture, Holly and Phil began to laugh after she said: 'It has got some girth, I'll give him that.'
