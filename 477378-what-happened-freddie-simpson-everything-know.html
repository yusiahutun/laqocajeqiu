Who is Freddie Simpson from A League of Their Own?

Fredda Marie Simpson, alias Freddie Simpson, is a former actress and model. She is also an athlete, business owner, yoga guru, and acupuncturist. She came into the limelight during her acting days but she leads a more private life now.

How old is Freddie Simpson?

Freddie Simpson's age is 59 years as of June 2023. She was born on 10th August 1963, and her Zodiac sign is Leo.

Where is Freddie Simpson from?

Actress Freddie Simpson was born in Louisville, Kentucky, United States of America, and was raised in northern Kentucky. She resides in Charlottesville, Virginia, United States of America. Her nationality is American, and her ethnicity is White.

Early years

At four, Freddie developed an interest in athletics. She became a self-styled playboy at a tender age.

She spent most of her summers in her teenagehood at sports camps in northern Kentucky. She was actively involved in softball, volleyball, and basketball.

Educational background

The former actress went to Villa Madonna Academy outside Covington, Kentucky, and graduated in 1981. She played various sports in high school.

Her excellent performance in sports earned her an athletic scholarship to the University of Kentucky. She played softball in Kentucky and Ohio. She dropped out of the University of Kentucky in her senior year to pursue other interests.

She later joined Yo San University in Southern California, where she pursued a programme in Traditional Chinese Medicine, Acupuncture and Herbology. She subsequently obtained a Master's Degree from the institution and graduated top of her class with distinctive honours.

Career

After dropping out of the University of Kentucky, Freddie worked on a tobacco farm. She soon got tired of the job and decided to pursue acting and modelling.

Modelling and acting career

Freddie signed with Prestige Modelling Agency. The agency helped her in bagging roles in several commercials, including a Nissan ad.

She also had a private practice in Venice Beach, California. She relocated to Virginia in May 2011 after spending two and a half decades in the Los Angeles area. She worked as an acupuncturist and registered massage therapist until July 2015.

What happened to Freddie Simpson?

After retiring from employment, the former actress started her own business. She runs an acupuncture company outside Charlottesville, Virginia.

Besides offering acupuncture sessions, she is a yogi who practices and teaches healing yoga and qigong. She offers therapeutic services, including salt and sound therapies.

Additionally, she offers diet and nutrition education and counselling to her clients. She has a dedicated website people can use to learn more about her work and book service appointments.
