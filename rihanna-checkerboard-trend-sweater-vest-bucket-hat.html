Rihanna Embraces Checkerboard Trend in Sweater Vest &amp; Bucket Hat

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  359 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Rihanna loves a good hat trend.

Last Thursday, the songstress was photographed leaving boyfriend, rapper, and fellow “fashion killa” A$AP Rocky’s house in Los Angeles sporting a look featuring the style set’s favorite print as of late: checkerboard. Going viral on social media sites like Instagram, the retro 1950s motif has shown up in many different forms, but for anyone interested in a more laidback approach, Rihanna’s latest ensemble is worth pinning to your mood board. Wearing a sold-out green-and-purple crocheted bucket hat by celeb-favorite knit brand Memorial Day (with her revived pixie cut peeking out from underneath), she completed the look with a lavender sweater vest and lime-green vinyl pants from Martine Rose.

Leaning in to the playfulness of her off-duty outfit, the beauty brand founder donned multiple rows of colorful beaded necklaces and bracelets. To give the statement-making look an elevated feel, she also added a structured tote from Givenchy and her favorite white point-toe pumps by Amina Muaddi. Rihanna topped off her vintage-inspired look with oversized, oval-shaped sunglasses, further embracing the outfit’s mod aesthetic.

A fan of the complementary color palette, Rihanna sported her latest look not long after the singer posted a string of photos to her Instagram page, spotlighting unreleased pieces from her lingerie line, Savage x Fenty. She posed in a lilac coordinate boxer set emblazoned with her brand’s logo, with neon-green socks from Erl and a pair of chunky white loafers by Gucci. Making a strong case for the unexpected pairing, the style maven who is notorious for mixing and matching, is keeping the streak going, both on and off duty.
