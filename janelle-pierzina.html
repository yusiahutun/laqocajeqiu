Janelle Pierzina is one of the most beloved houseguests of the reality TV show 'Big Brother.' As of 2020, Minnesota native has been in the show for four times.

Janelle Pierzina and her Husband Jess DeSanto, Were College Sweethearts

Janelle Pierzina first met her husband, Jess DeSanto, when she was studying at the University of Minnesota Duluth. In a 2018 Instagram Post, the reality TV star stated:

“I know I’m a day late, but #happynationalspouseday. I met my husband when I was 18 so I’ve known him longer in life than I haven’t!”

Pierzina and her husband, after dating for years, finally got engaged in September 2009 and got married in June 2010. Soon after the marriage, the couple started a family. 

Janelle Pierzina with her husband and Children. IMAGE SOURCE: Janelle Pierzina's Instagram

The pair's first child Violet Anne DeSanto was born in December 2011. Similarly, the couple welcomed a son Lincoln William DeSanto in August 2013. The couple's youngest child, Stella DeSanto, was born in September 2014. In addition to that, Pierzina and her husband consider their home rabbit as their child as well.

Janelle Pierzina Was Big Brother Contestant Four Times; Learn About Her Net Worth

Pierzina has been part of the reality TV Show Big Brother four times. She first participated in Big Brother 6 in 2005, then Big Brother: All-Stars in 2006, Big Brother 14 in 2012, and Big Brother 22 in 2020. Though she has never won any season, she has always been liked by viewers. In fact, she won America's Favorite Houseguest award.

Besides Big Brother, she has competed on the 31st season of The Amazing Race with her Big Brother bestie Britney Haynes. They got eliminated very early in the show.

Before entering reality TV, Pierzina worked as an actress, model, and cocktail waitress. Currently, she works as a real estate agent in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Her net worth is $1 million.
