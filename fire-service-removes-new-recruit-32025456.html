Fire service removes new recruit post after pervy blokes offer to 'smash her doors in'

 Posted on May 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  518 words  |  Martina Birk

A fire service has removed a post welcoming a new recruit from social media after it was inundated with inappropriate comments.

The original post on X (formerly Twitter) welcomed a new firefighter, Millie, to Wareham Fire Station after she completed her basic training. It featured a snap of the new recruit grinning in her uniform.

The post said: "Meet Millie, Millie has just joined Wareham Fire Station and has just past (sic) her basic training only last week. Millie's favourite Tool is the big red key, which allows Mille (sic) to smash through doors."
