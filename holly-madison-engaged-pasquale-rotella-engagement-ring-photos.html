Holly Madison Engaged to Pasquale Rotella -- Check Out Her BIG Ring!

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  199 words  |  Martina Birk

It's wedding bells after baby for Holly Madison!

The reality TV star and boyfriend/baby daddy Pasquale Rotella got engaged over the weekend during the Electric Daisy Carnival (aka EDC) -- and we've got photos of the proposal and her ginormous ring.

"I got engaged on Sunday," Holly shared on her official blog Monday. "My fiancé was so wonderful in creating such a romantic night! He proposed to me at the top of the Ferris wheel at EDC Las Vegas. I was so happy that so many of our close friends were there to share the wonderful moment with us!"

Rotella is the founder of the Las Vegas rave and the two were spotted sharing PDA there all weekend long, even posing for some photos with daughter Rainbow Aurora in the quieter, backstage areas.

"I couldn't have asked for a more wonderful or magical proposal," she adds.

Before popping the question, the couple had already picked out an engagement date -- and will reportedly tie the knot on August 14.
