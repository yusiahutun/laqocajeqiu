Big Machine Label Group &amp; Hard 8 Working Group Partner To Launch The Hard Working Record Company

 Posted on May 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  267 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Back L-R: BMLG’s Mike Rittberg, HWRC’s LJ Stoll & BMLG’s Andrew Kautz (Front L-R) Hemsath, Borchetta & Egan

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) – Big Machine Label Group (BMLG) and Hard 8 Working Group (H8WG) are partnering to form The Hard Working Record Company. The new label venture, made by BMLG President/CEO/Founder Scott Borchetta and H8WG co-founders Dirk Hemsath and Rich Egan, along with partners David Conway and Mike Bachta, will expand each of their scopes and open doors to discover, develop and publish Pop musical acts and beyond.

LJ Stoll joins the new company as General Manager (GM) and Vice President (VP) of A&R. He comes from Se Lavi Productions/Rebel Music, where he led the A&R team.

The union of BMLG and H8WG progressed naturally as they had worked together previously on Brantley Gilbert and Kidd G. Based in Nashville, the new label under the BMLG umbrella combines the resources of both groups to launch and amplify new artists and their music.

Borchetta said, “Our continued growth and dominance depend on the quality and visionary A&R in all genres. The Hard Working team live for artist discovery and development, and they’re bringing in heat from the onset.”

Hemsath added, “Rich and I had been toying with the idea of starting a label again since that’s the world we both came from. We had been working closely with Scott and Big Machine on the artist Kidd G, and Scott mentioned he wanted to start a more Pop-leaning imprint, and that’s the world we’ve been in heavily for the last few years, most recently building Pop star Tate McRae.”
