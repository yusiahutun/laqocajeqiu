Combining classic style with modern conveniences

 Posted on April 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  367 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Harbourside, consisting of three connected towers of 80 storeys each, is located in West Kowloon, and provides 1,122 apartments with harbour views.

Developed by Hang Lung Properties, the apartments range from two-bedroom units of about 1,000 sqft to four-bedroom penthouses of about 2,200 sqft. The four-bedroom penthouses and duplexes on the top floors of the three towers range from 2,300 to 3,000 sqft.

Each unit has a master bedroom suite and a versatile stand-alone multipurpose room. About two-thirds of the units are designed with a 50 sqft balcony. All the four-bedroom penthouses and duplexes are equipped with a Teuco steam bath, Tylo sauna, EuroCave wine cellar and Tutondo's interactive whole-house music system.

Kitchens are fitted with Bulthaup cabinets, a Gaggenau electric stone grill, Siemens steamer, an oven and built-in dishwasher, a Costel LCD screen, and radio and hands-free phone. Intelligence features are infused into Harbourside. The Cytech automated home system simplifies the control of air conditioning, lights, security and communication, while also monitoring for gas leaks and fire hazards.

All entrances to the residential lifts and common areas are guarded by the Octopus card enable entry system, which allows residents access to the premises and also functions as a pre-paid card for access to the public transport network.

Through the Harbourside's webpage, residents can access up-to-the-minute information on various estate announcements, as well as place bookings for clubhouse facilities.

Club Harbourside's common facilities are on the fifth floor, and they include a total of 35 different areas.

The Fitness Terrain packs in a gymnasium, massage rooms, jacuzzis, steam rooms and saunas.

The juice bar is located in Cosmo Oasis which includes harbour-view function rooms for dining or entertaining, complemented by a pre-function lounge and a cigar room. Other attractions include a communal computer area, billiards, two karaoke rooms, a golf simulator, a 16-seat digital theatre, two piano rooms, a reading room and a library.

The Children's Empire features a video lounge, play areas, games room, computer room, art corner and a table tennis room.

The Harbourside is located above the Airport Express Kowloon station which is accessible by MTR and Airport Express. The West Kowloon Expressway and Western Harbour Crossing also make travel convenient for residents.
