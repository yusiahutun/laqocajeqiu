Kristen Stewart Wears Sheer Bra and Garters Out in NYC

 Posted on March 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  227 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kristen Stewart's press tour for Love Lies Bleeding continues, and produced yet another hard hitting look while she was out in New York City.

The actress arrived at Late Night With Seth Meyers on March 13, wearing an all black look with lingerie details. The 33-year-old paired a sheer, netted bra with a fitted black skirt that she accessorized with black thigh high stockings held up by black garters. To add a cool girl edge, Stewart added a black leather jacket over the ensemble and completed the look with black stilettos and red tinted sunglasses.

The look is the latest in a series of leg-bearing ensembles. On March 12, the Twilight star wore a beige Brunello Cucinelli look that consisted of a undone button-up blouse with matching knit underwear that added a pop of color with brown piping. Stewart wasn't totally bare-legged — she finished off the look with sheer tights and chocolate-colored suede pumps, and accessorized with a quilted black Chanel handbag.

The lingerie elements aren't new, either — for the red carpet premiere of Love Lies Bleeding, Stewart appeared at the event in a bodysuit by Cosabella, which featured thin straps and bold cut out details. Instead of opting for pants, she completed the look with black sheer tights and black pumps.

Needless to say, Stewart is officially in her cool girl era.
