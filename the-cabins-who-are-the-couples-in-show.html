    Mamudo and Robyn

    Mamudo and Robyn arrived in the Cabin last week and seemingly got on like a house on fire, especially after Mamudo's sweet gesture of bringing flowers. The pair had one or two hiccups, but eventually left the cabin as an 'exclusive couple'. We have high hopes for these two!

    Engineer Remi and model Charlotte hit it off straight away after they joined the ITV show this week. The pair decided to stay together overnight after a romantic hot-tub date. However, Thursday's episode teases trouble in paradise for this couple.

    Sarah and Charlotte

    Sarah and Charlotte hit it off immediately. The pair shared a number of nights together as a pair and eventually left after meeting the maximum time for their stay. The two left as an "exclusive couple" and have teased their romance on social media since.

    Tom and Olivia are another couple that spent the duration of their time in together. The two had an instant spark and ended up leaving together as a couple. We'll soon find out if they went the distance on the reunion episode on the 19th!
