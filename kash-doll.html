Kash Doll is a famous rapper from the United States of America. Kash Doll is famous for her singles “For Everybody” and “Ice Me Out” around the world. She is signed with the recording label Republic Reports currently.

Early Life

Kash Doll was born in Detroit, Michigan on the 14th of March 1992. Kash’s zodiac sign is Pisces and she is currently 31 years old. Her real birth name is Arkeisha Antoinette Knight. Kash is of African-American descent and has American nationality. There is no information about Kash’s parent’s identity at the moment.

Kash has faced some tough days during her childhood. Furthermore, Kash’s father died when she was just a year old. Kash’s mother got remarried after some time. Moreover, Kash’s mother and stepfather never had a good relationship and argued constantly with each other. Kash had to bear some of the financial burdens of the family due to being the eldest among her five siblings. She took various odd jobs since her younger days because of financial conditions.

Kash found little time for herself where she discovered her passion for freestyle and writing lyrics in between her work. Kash studied business management at Henry Ford College. However, Kash left college to support her family.

Professional life

Kash Doll started working various odd jobs in her younger days. She started her career as a dancer in the adult entertainment industry after she left college to support her family. Kash started working as a rapper for nightclub patrons soon. She also started to perform in events, local clubs, and school programs too. Kash wanted to explore more in the sector after she identified her talent as a rapper. She later signed a deal with a local recording house which became a failure.

Kash later started posting her rap video on her social media including Instagram. Drake invited her to perform with him on the Summer Sixteen tour after he saw her videos. Kash started her career by releasing a remix version of Tinashe’s “2 On” in 2014. She dropped her first single on the cover of AV’s “Run Me My Money” in 2015 which received good reviews.

Kash released her first mixtape titled “K.R.E.A.M. Kash Rules Everything Around Me” the same year after her first single became a hit. The same year, Kash signed a record deal with BMB Records and released the “Keisha Vs Kash Doll” mixtape which featured artists like Zaytoven, Trina, Lyrica Anderson, and B.O.B. Kash released her third mixtape “Trapped in The Dollhouse” in 2016, and her first extended play “Kashin’ Out” through the RMMR record label in the same year.

On July 16, 2018, Kash launched the Ep “The Vault” which consists of the song “For Everybody”. Kash released her debut album “Stacked” under the recording label Republic in 2019. Moreover, Kash also worked with Iggy Azalea on her single “Fuck It Up” in 2019. Besides that, Kash made her cinematic debut in the drama film “Birds of a Feather 2” in 2018.

Achievements
