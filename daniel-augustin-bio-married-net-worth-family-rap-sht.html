Meet Actor Daniel Augustin! His Bio, Married, Net Worth, Family

Coming from the creator of HBO’s hit series Insecure Issa Rae, Rap Sh!t, an HBO Max original, gives people a look into the female rap industry. The cast of the show has some huge names as well as some emerging ones. Daniel Augustin, who most recently starred in Hulu’s How I Met Your Father, is also a part of the show.

Learn about Daniel’s role on HBO Max’s Rap Sh!t, and about his net worth, married life, and more in the wiki article below.

Daniel Augustin On HBO Max’s Rap Sh!t

The creator of Rap Sh!t shared with The Hollywood Reporter that she conceived the idea for the show while she was still working on Insecure. The new HBO Max show follows two former high school friends who unexpectedly form a rap group.

American actor Daniel Augustin plays the role of Maurice in Rap Sh!t. Maurice is Shawna Clark’s funny co-worker and Shawna (played by Aida Osman) is an ambitious rapper from Miami waiting for her big break. Both Maurice and Shawn hate to work at their hotel jobs but Maurice somehow passed the time by shamelessly flirting with the female guests.

Daniel Augustin is the perfect casting for a show that takes its viewers into the rap scene considering the actor himself had become a hip-hop recording artist by the age of ten. At such a young age, Daniel had started a rap group at his church Agape Church of God. At age 21, Rap Sh!t star even recorded his first solo, Neo-Soul project titled: “I’m Sorry 4 the Delay.”

Daniel Augustin Net Worth

Talking about Daniel Augustin’s career in the entertainment industry, he got his start as an actor in 2015 through the TV Mini-series Treble in Paradise, in which he played the role of Danny.

From 2018 to 2019, Daniel landed brief acting roles in many projects including Insatiable, Wu-Tang: An American Saga, The Resident, PillowTalk, I Am Frankie, among others.

In late 2019, Daniel landed a recurring role in the TV series David Makes Man. Some of his popular recent credits include Grey’s Anatomy and Dynasty.

Most recently, he starred in the How I Met Your Mother spin-off series, How I Met Your Father. Daniel landed the role of Ian in the Hulu original during recasting. God Friended Me star, Brandon Michael Hall, was originally cast in the role but had to exit due to a scheduling conflict.

Daniel is represented by Pantheon and CGEM Talent.

According to resumes.actorsaccess.com, Daniel trained himself as an actor by taking different training programs. Some of the training programs that he took are Improv 101, Animator Voiceover, Stunt Boot Camp, Total Prep I, 10 Week, and 4 Week Audition Course, to name a few.

As per Daniel’s LinkedIn handle, he is a Television Director at Kinfolk-Augustin Productions. Previously, he worked as a freelance video editor at God’s Image Films, as a Production Assistant at As Seen On TV, Inc, and as a Television Director at Cake: the Series – Augustin Productions.

Is Daniel Augustin Married?

Actor Daniel Augustin is happily married to an actress named Sh’Kia. According to Sh’Kia Augustin’s Facebook, the two exchanged wedding vows on April 28, 2018. On May 10, 2018, Daniel’s wife first shared a picture from their wedding event on her IG. The picture was of Daniel kissing Sh’Kia’s forehead as she closed her eyes.

Besides being an incredible actress, Daniel’s wife is also a writer, producer, and single. Some of her acting credits include Kold x Windy, Kinfolk: The Series, Black Lightning, and What’s More Important? among others.

According to Sh’Kia’s LinkedIn handle, she currently works at Augustin Productions as an Executive Producer/Screenwriter. She completed her Bachelor’s Degree in Finance at Florida Atlantic University. Sh’Kia also studied at Florida Atlanta University.

The actor and his partner, Sh’Kia, together worked in a series called Cake: The Series. Daniel was the director of the show whereas Sh’Kia was the writer.

Daniel Augustin Family

Actor Daniel Augustin was born to his parents Georges Augustin Sr (father), born in Gonaives Haiti, and Alida Augustin (mother), born in Cap Haitien Haiti. Unfortunately, Daniel’s father is not here with us in this world.

Daniel also has three siblings, all sisters, in the family. Ruth Augustin and Naomie Matilus Augustin are two of his sisters’ names. Naomie is a married woman, a mother of two, and an Instructional Reading Coach at the School District of Palm Beach County.

How Old Is Daniel Augustin?

Daniel Augustin Height
