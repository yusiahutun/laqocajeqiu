How to Make Ranch Pasta Tuna Salad

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  131 words  |  Martina Birk

Creamy ranch dressing adds the perfect tang to this portable pasta meal.

Serving Size:

4

Active Time:

20 min.

Total Time:

20 min.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups yogurt ranch dressing, such as Bolthouse Farms
  • 2 Tbs. minced fresh oregano
  • 2 Tbs. minced fresh parsley
  • 2 Tbs. minced scallions
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 4 cups cooked small shell pasta, such as Ronzoni
  • 1 cup sliced red peppers
  • 1 (12 oz.) can solid tuna packed in water, drained
  • 8 cups torn baby romaine leaves

Instructions

  • In bowl, mix first 4 ingredients, 2 tsp. lemon juice and 1 tsp. lemon zest; stir in pasta to coat.
  • Divide pasta evenly among four ½ to 1 liter jars or containers. Divide remaining ingredients among jars, layering as desired; close jars.
