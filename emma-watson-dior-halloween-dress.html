Emma Watson Wears the Perfect Dior Dress for Halloween

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  219 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Getty Images

While other celebrities dressed up as everything from life-size Barbies to Christina Aguilera on Halloween, Emma Watson forewent the costume and stepped out in Dior. But the choice of attire was still nonetheless festive—just look at the bats, bees and other whimsical creatures embellishing the sheer number.

The dress is freshly plucked from the Spring 2017 runway in Paris, but Watson made some chic alterations by adding a sheer neckline and nude slip underneath the skirt and bodice. She topped off the look with Jessica McCormack jewelry to attend Harper's BAZAAR UK'S Women of the Year Awards.

Imaxtree/Getty

Aside from its fitting Halloween motifs, the gown is perfect for Watson for another reason: the piece is a part of Maria Grazia Chiuri's inaugural Dior collection, one that included "We Should All Be Feminists" t-shirts and celebrated the first time a woman was appointed as artist director for the French house. It's only right that Watson, a renowned young feminist and UN Women Goodwill Ambassador, step out in it.

We hope she wears one of those t-shirts next.
