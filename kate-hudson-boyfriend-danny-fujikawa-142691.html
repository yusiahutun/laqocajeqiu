Kate Hudson Gushes Over Boyfriend Danny Fujikawa on Their Anniversary: Its Been Truly Incredib

 Posted on May 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Larita Shotwell

She’s a lady in love! Actress Kate Hudson couldn’t help but gush over her new boyfriend, musician Danny Fujikawa, on their “first date anniversary.”

“The first time I met Danny I was 23 and enormously pregnant with Ryder. His step sisters are my best friends @sarafoster @erinfoster and so it goes we have been in the same circle for over a decade!” Watch the video below to see what else she wrote about him on Instagram!

This is hardly the first time the How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days star publicly gushed over her man. Kate couldn’t help but talk about her beau during the premiere of her latest flick Marshall. In the past, the Mother’s Day star has been romantically linked to Nick Jonas, Alex Rodriguez, and Owen Wilson.

“He’s standing very nicely, waiting. He’s the best! I’ve known him a long time,” she told Entertainment Tonight. The pair were first rumored to be dating back in March and walked the red carpet as a couple for the first time two months later. A source previously told People of the relationship, “Kate’s a free spirit who has always been attracted to musicians, but Danny also appeals to her because he is talented, bright and cosmopolitan like she is. Kate’s enjoying herself. At this point, it’s not serious.” However, we would love to see her settle down with the NYU grad who owns his own record label!

(Photo Credit: Splash)

“My cute man. He’s the best!” she previously told Extra — then when asked what she loved most about him, she candidly replied, “How long do you have? He’s a good man.”

Danny also received the seal of approval from his girlfriend’s mother, Goldie Hawn, and her longtime boyfriend, Kurt Russell. Back in May, he told [Entertainment Tonight](http://www.etonline.com/news/217236exclusivekurtrussellgoldiehawnapproveofkatehudsonredcarpetdebutwithboyfrienddannyfujikawa), “He’s a nice guy, Danny. Yeah, I like him. I always approve. Kate brings good guys.” The Snatched star added, “He’s a great guy. Yeah, he’s very special actually.” How sweet!

Kate also has two sons from previous relationships — a 13-year-old, Ryder, with her ex-husband, Chris Robinson, and a six-year-old, Bingham, with her ex-fiancé, Matthew Bellamy. We’re sure they love Danny as much as their mom does!
