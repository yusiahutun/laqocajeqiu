Latest wooden bed designs in Kenya

 Posted on February 27, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  967 words  |  Larita Shotwell

What is the first thing that comes to your mind after a long, dull or hectic day? A bed, since it gives you a chance to relax, right? This implies that it should be comfortable enough. So, how many wooden bed designs do you know that can give you the best service ever in Kenya?

A good bed has always been the best companion for everyone, whether spending some happy moments, shedding tears or just taking a break. In the past, beds were either purely metallic or simple pattern wooden ones. Technological advancements saw carpenters come with more ideologies and now beds are available in different sizes shapes, styles and even colours. Here are some best and latest wooden bed designs you will long to have in your bedroom.

1. Cradle bed

For new mothers, here is a nice bed for your son or daughter. The bed is made of wood and can host two small children at the same time. Mothers can move the bed as close to their main bed as they wish to keep the babies near them in case of anything. Vertical firm wooden bars are surrounding the bed to make sure your baby is safe. The upper part is kept open for easy access by the mother.

2. Water bed

Water bed is one of the latest bed designs in Kenya this year. It has a mattress filled with cold or warm water depending on the climate of your region. The bed best serves patients facing specific disorders like cancer. When filled with cold water, it gives them a comfortable sleep. In coastal areas, when filled with cold water, the bed provides a cooling effect to the person sleeping on it.

3. Queen bed

Do you love multi-purpose beds? Queen bed designs come with multiple draws in the head and footer area. The draws can be used to store sensitive documents, antique items or any of your belongings. There are also small closets in the bed where you can keep unwanted things in them. With its inside fitting and extra comfortable mattress, the bed is very soft and guarantees you solace. Also, the bed is made from hardwood making it durable.

4. Antique bed

Do you love antique wooden furniture in your bedroom? If you do, then this bed with surely grab your whole heart at first sight. Made from fine hard timber the bed will last for over ten years. The fascinating thing about the bed is its crown-like design on the head side. It also has a mini roof which adds more beauty to this lovely design.

5. Twaddle bed

Looking for a nice bed for your kids? Twaddle beds are always beautiful and designed childishly to give your children a secure and playful item. With its charming and very colourful decorations designs, any kid will surely love the bed. The bottom area is also designed in such a way that it gives your child a playing ground while the upper mainly functions as a bed.

6. Bunk bed

Are you currently looking for a wooden design bed that will host more than two people in one small room? Bunk beds are always the best choice. They are long-lasting and designed like double beds. The lower bed is much bigger than the upper and can host more than one person.

7. Canopy bed

Do you love unique, beautiful, classy designs? This home bed is a perfect match for you. It is surrounded by four posts which hold curtain decorations from all the sides. During the night the curtains are closed to ensure that no one peeps through to see you. This is especially nice for newlywed couples.

8. Futon bed

For snug chair lovers, here is a wooden bed made with the best design ever. It allows you to rest while you are reading newspapers, novels or working with your laptop right in your living/sitting room. The bed design is similar to that of beach beds. However, it is made with thick mattress cushions to ensure you are extra comfortable.

9. Wood frame bed

This is an example of a simple bed which is modified with wooden frames to look better. The mattress has also been framed within the bed to give it an embossed look. The head part is fitted with a cushion that gives you comfort when you want to rest your head while seated upright.

10. Flatbed

We all love the igneous beds that were used back in the golden era. A similar bed made from wood is here. It is square and big enough to accommodate at least three people. The flatbed doesn’t have any supporters in any side making it fit to be placed in the middle of any room. Also, it doesn’t have many decorations. Therefore, it is suitable for men since they don’t love them.
