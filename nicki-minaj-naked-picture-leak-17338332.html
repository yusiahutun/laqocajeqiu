Nicki Minaj naked pic leak: Troll posing as rapper's ex shares explicit images

 Posted on October 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  240 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A Twitter account impersonating the rapper's ex-boyfriend Safaree Samuels began to tweet several X-rated images claiming to be Nicki on Wednesday.

Along with messages including "I broke up with her", the account @ScaffBeezy shared pictures of a topless woman with hair covering her modesty.

The false Twitter account also published other explicit pictures showing a woman's naked body in sexual situations.

There is no confirmation that these pictures are of Miss Minaj, and it would appear that a troll is targeting the star.

Nicki's ex-boyfriend, Safaree, spoke out to distance himself from the scandal, assuring fans that he was in no way involved with the leak.

He tweeted: "It's a shame I have to even address this dumb s*** but I have 1 Twitter and it's this 1 that is verified! For those of you who can't read…learn."

Safaree's official account, @IAMSAFAREE, has 148,000 followers, while the fake account tweeting the explicit images has less than 4,000.

TMZ reported that a rep for Samuels could not confirm whether the pictures were real, and that the record producer has moved on from Nicki after they split last year after dating for nearly 14 years.

This scandal comes as Nicki announced her engagement to rapper Meek Mill yesterday just days after confirming their romance.

The Anaconda star shared a picture of her heart-shaped yellow diamond ring, rumoured to be worth $500,000.

The Daily Star Online has contacted Nicki's representative for comment.
