Who is Kirk Hammett?

Kirk Lee Hammett is an American writer and musician known for his role as the lead guitarist and writer in the heavy metal band Metallica. Kirk was born in San Francisco, California, on November 28, 1962.

His parents are Teofila Chefela Oyao(a Filipino) and Dennis L. Hammett (a German-Scottish-Irish merchant mariner). The popular guitarist attended De Anza High School in Richmond, California, and after graduating, he furthered his studies at San Francisco State University.
