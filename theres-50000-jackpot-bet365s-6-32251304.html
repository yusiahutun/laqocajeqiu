Theres a 50,000 jackpot in bet365s 6 Horses Challenge, as Newbury, Kelso and Doncaster offer

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1044 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The bet365 6 Horses Challenge once again offers up a free shot at its £50,000 jackpot for players that can predict six correct winners. Register, pick your winners, and potentially win huge prizes; it’s that simple.

Last weekend’s free-to-play round didn’t see a jackpot winner in the parade ring, with no one able to celebrate six correct picks in their selections. However, a staggering total of 49 players managed to correctly predict 5 picks. Leaving them with a confirmed share of the £10,000 consolation prize, with others taking home prizes for their 4 correct guesses.

This week provides another opportunity for players to put their knowledge to the test and potentially win big, it’s exactly the same premise for this weekend’s edition, with four guesses giving a share of £5,000, five correct guesses offering a share of a cool £10,000, with that £50,000 on offer to those who get a perfect six winners picked.

Newbury, Kelso and Doncaster are set to host the races for this weeks 6 Horses Challenge, as players look to achieve perfection in this week’s edition.

This week’s races consist of an even split between all 3 destinations, kicking off at 1.20pm at Newbury with a Class 2 Chase, with the 1.55pm also featuring from the Berkshire racecourse. Racing from north of the border at Kelso falls either side of the aforementioned, with the inclusion of the 1.42pm and 2.50pm.


We then head to Doncaster to round out this week’s edition of the 6 Horses Challenge with it featuring the fifth and sixth race slots. The races 3.10pm and 3.40pm provide the finale for the players to achieve the £50,000 jackpot.
