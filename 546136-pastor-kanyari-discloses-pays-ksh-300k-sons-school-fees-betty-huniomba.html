  • Pastor Kanyari has announced plans to get into marriage again and unveiled plans he has for his dream to come true
  • He also opened up about co-parenting with Betty Bayo and how much he pays for his son's school fees
  • Social media users commended him for thinking of marriage, while others vowed to shoot their shots

Renowned preacher Pastor Victor Kanyari has opened up about educating his children and the partnership that he has with his ex-wife, Betty Bayo.

Kanyari noted how proud he was of his kids and revealed that his ex always calls him to pay school fees for their son.

Where Kanyari will look for new wife

While on a TikTok live, the controversial preacher also admitted plans he had to re-marry and enjoy a sweet marriage.

"I was married to Betty Bayo, and together, we had two children, Sky and Dani. Sky is in America and Dani is schooling. Right now, their mum has called me and asked me for the kids' school fees. I asked him how much she wanted, and she said KSh 350,000. That is what I pay for my son's fees in a private school," he said.

He also unveiled plans to find himself a beautiful woman on TikTok to be his wife and noted that he has no intention of dating for a long time.

"I want to marry again. And I will marry from TikTok. I will find a good lady from TikTok. It's not a must for people to know each other, there is nothing like getting to know each other. If you love each other, you go for it," he added.
