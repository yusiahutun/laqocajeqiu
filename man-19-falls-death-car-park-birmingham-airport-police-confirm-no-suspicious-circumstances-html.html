Man, 19, falls to his death from car park at Birmingham Airport as police confirm no suspicious circ

 Posted on March 11, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  158 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

A teenager has died after falling from a car park at Birmingham Airport.

Despite efforts from emergency services, the teenager, aged 19, died from his injuries.

The death is not being treated as suspicious and police have confirmed they are not looking for anyone in connection to the incident.

West Midlands Police and paramedics were called just after 3pm on Friday, October 6.

Emergency services were called to Birmingham Airport just after 3pm on Friday, October 6. Pictured, stock photo 

They said nothing could be done to save the man's life. 

A force spokesman said: 'We were called after a 19-year-old man fell from a car park at Birmingham Airport just after 3pm on Friday 6 October.

'Sadly, despite receiving treatment, nothing could done to save the man and he died from his injuries.

'There are no suspicious circumstances and a coroner's report is being completed.'

A file is being prepared for Birmingham and Solihull Coroner's Court.
