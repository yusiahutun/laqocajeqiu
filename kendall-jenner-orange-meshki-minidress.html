Kendall Jenner Wears a Tiny, Sparkly Minidress by Meshki

 Posted on May 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  331 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Kendall Jenner sported a minidress by Australian brand Meshki for a night on vacation in Greece.
  • Costing $57, the shiny number won't break the bank.
  • Jenner packed a few cute swimsuits for her getaway as well.

It's no surprise that Kendall Jenner's got an enviable vacation wardrobe, but what does come as a shock is that it's more affordable than expected. The model stepped out in Mykonos, Greece earlier this week wearing a shimmery minidress by the Australian brand Meshki that only costs $57 (79 AUD).

Jenner paired the leggy, spaghetti-strap frock with white Adidas continental sneakers, gold earrings, and a crystal-covered mini shoulder bag.

Funnily enough, the designers behind Meshki have actually used Jenner's wardrobe as style inspiration. "We look at what LA celebrities are wearing — the Jenners, the Hadids — and then put our own twist on it," co-owner Shadi told Racked in 2017. With Kendall stepping out in one of the brand's creations, things have come full circle.

In addition to the flirty Meshki number, it appears Jenner went heavy on the swimsuits while packing for her Mykonos getaway. Given she and her pals spent a lot of time on boats and on the beach, according to their dreamy Instagrams, the bathing suit dress code makes perfect sense.

For one outing, she wore a ribbed lilac skirt with a Kira Kira-friendly bedazzled bikini bottom. (Swipe right on her Instagram.)

While hopping on the #BottleCapChallenge, she zoomed on a jet ski wearing a neon green two-piece.

And while catching some shade, she lounged in a bright yellow bikini.

We're taking notes for the next time we pack for vacation.
