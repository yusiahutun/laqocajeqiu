Fort Lauderdale palatial estate of Wayne Huizenga on sale for $27m

 Posted on March 6, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  719 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The palatial riverside estate of billionaire sports team owner H Wayne Huizenga is on sale for $27 million months after his death in March.

His landmark Fort Lauderdale property, one of the biggest in the city, has eight bedrooms and nine bathrooms arranged in separate wings for family, guests, and staff. 

Every room in the 18,000 square-foot house, designed in a mixture of French and Mediterranean styles, is fabulously decorated with marble, tiles, and wooden finishing.
