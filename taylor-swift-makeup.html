Her Red Lip &amp; Winged Liner, Unpacked

If you’re not-so-patiently waiting to watch Taylor Swift tear it up on stage at The Eras Tour, now’s the best time to figure out your concert beat. And what better way to do this than to go straight to the source? While Taylor has always been pretty secretive about what she puts on her face, thanks to Pat McGrath, we all can have a cheeky little peek into the makeup she wears.

Pat McGrath Labs previously joined forces with Taylor to launch ‘Taylor-Made’ kits that are comprised of the singer’s fave products from the brand. These kits, along with the celebrity makeup artist and beauty brand founder’s Instagram posts that detail Taylor’s looks, are all you need to try your hand at copying Taylor Swift’s makeup.

So buckle up because there are plenty of options to choose from based on the singer’s makeup look in her “Bejeweled” music video.

I don’t know about you (*wink, wink*), but I bet you’ll want to add all of the following seven makeup products to your cart so you can twin with TSwift while you watch her perform on stage. But also, why not make a bold red lip and fierce winged liner an everyday thing? Get Taylor’s signature look to look enchanted, delicate, gorgeous, fearless—basically, everything the singer is and more.

  • Pat McGrath Labs Liquid Lipstick

    I’m sure everyone and their grandma wants to know what lip product Taylor uses to achieve her iconic red lip, and I’m here to let you know that it’s the LiquiLUST Legendary Wear Matte Lipstick in Elson 4. Her go-to shade keeps going in and out of stock at Sephora, but it’s currently available on Pat McGrath’s site. This is definitely a run, don’t walk situation.

  • Pat McGrath Labs Liquid Eyeliner

    Taylor Swift’s winged liner always looks so sharp and precise. As someone who struggles to draw those little flicks, I’ll definitely be checking out this liquid eyeliner.

  • Pat McGrath Labs Blush

    For a silk-velvet finish and gorgeous packaging, tap Pat McGrath’s powder blush onto the apples of your cheeks. It provides medium coverage and a softly diffused hint of color.

  • Pat McGrath Labs Mascara

    Taylor’s eye makeup is definitely the focus of her “Bejeweled” look, and I personally can’t stop looking at her long lashes. That’s all thanks to Pat McGrath Labs’ cult-favorite mascara, which lengthens and defines lashes while also conditioning, strengthening, and hydrating them.

  • Pat McGrath Labs Lip Liner

    We all know applying a bright red lip can be tricky to ace, but this waterproof, transfer-resistant, lip pencil is here to lend you a helping hand. The exact shades that TSwift wore—Blood Lust and Deep Dive—might not be available at the moment, but Suburbia (a mid-tone rose) is a close third.

  • Pat McGrath Labs Eyeshadow Palette

    From velvet mattes to shimmery metallics, you won’t run out of eye looks to try with this 10-shade palette. The shadows are silky smooth to the touch and blend seamlessly.

  • Pat McGrath Labs Highlighter Duo

    Taylor’s “Bejeweled” look was so radiant and soft-focus-looking that you know she had to have used a dewy highlighter. Pat McGrath’s Instagram post lets us know that this duo stick is responsible for illuminating the celeb’s skin to perfection.
