Mitchell Eason Biography, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Mitchell Eason is an American individual who gained popularity as a Reality TV star and social media influencer, having participated in the second season of Netflix’s show The Circle. With over 260,000 followers on his authenticated Instagram profile, he previously worked as a health insurance sales representative before landing the role.

He shares workout and modeling-related posts on Instagram and actively interacts with his audience via comments. His mother and brother were also part of the first season of The Circle, and Chloe Veitch joined him in the show’s second season.

Early Life

Mitchell Eason, currently 24 years old in 2023, was born on September 16th, 1999. He holds licenses and certifications as a Real Estate Salesperson and an Insurance Agent. He earned his degree in Python from Zero to Mastery Academy. Remarkably, he started his professional career at the age of eight in January 2007.

Mitchell Eason Career

Even though he was just 22 in 2021, Eason has already been an established model for several years, and his portfolio includes numerous trendy photoshoots. Remarkably, while still a high school senior, he even had the opportunity to walk in New York Fashion Week towards the end of 2016. Although Eason was successful as a model, he also embarked on a career as a real estate agent.

He considered real estate as one of his major interests and was a licensed realtor at Coldwell Banker Preferred for several years. Even though his mother still works as an agent at the same company, Eason is not presently listed on the website, indicating that he may have left. According to his LinkedIn profile, he is now a salesperson for Prudential Insurance. Eason’s initial venture into the business world involved creating his landscaping company during high school.

As a youngster, he had assisted his uncle’s company with some yard work, and this experience prompted him to establish Eason’s Scapes & Services at the age of 16. Eason showcased his new Beverly Hills mansion on his YouTube channel in a Cribs-style tour video. He mentioned that he had moved from Miami, where he worked as a bartender, to Los Angeles. In the video, Eason disclosed that he purchased the house for $33.2 million and gave a comprehensive tour of the property. Currently, on his LinkedIn profile, he identifies himself as self-employed with Netflix as his company.

The Perfect Match

Mitchell, known for his appearance on The Circle season 2, is set to return on Netflix in a new reality show called Perfect Match. Interestingly, Mitchell’s former girlfriend and fellow The Circle cast member, Chloe Veitch, will also participate in the show, which fans already know. Whether Mitchell and Chloe will rekindle their past romance on the show remains to be seen.

Social Media

Mitchell is a popular personality who has gained fame through his appearances on TV, small screen, and on Netflix. He maintains various public profiles on social media platforms such as Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube (@mitchelleason), TikTok, and more. In addition to being featured on IMDb for his work on shows like Perfect Match, The Circle, and The Shitsshow, he has also established a successful career as a realtor and insurance specialist. He has created a niche in these fields and built his own professional empire.
