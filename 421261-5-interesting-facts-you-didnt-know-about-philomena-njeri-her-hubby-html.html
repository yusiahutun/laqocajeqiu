Jonathan Mukundi and Philomena lived a perfect life that their peers admired. But behind the social media glamorous photos, all was not well at home.

Mukundi, a licensed gun-holder, shot Njeri three times in the head before turning the gun on himself. The lifeless bodies of the two were found inside their bedroom, which had been locked from inside on the evening of Tuesday, August 3.

1. Mukundi's marriage to Njeri

The two were married for 12 years, and many envied their union. They were a perfect match made in heaven.

2. They had no child.

3. Njeri left her matrimonial home a few months ago

According to sources, Njeri and Mukundi's marriage hit rock bottom recently, and they separated for three months and were not living together. They reunited on Monday, August 2, had a meeting to try to salvage their failed marriage.

Njeri and Mukundi ended up spending the night together at their Kirigiti home.

Sources also indicated that Njeri might have told Mukundi that she was pregnant for another man, and that's when drama ensued.

4. Mukundi was friends with Mugithi singer Samidoh Muchoki and several local celebrities. He stayed in the United States (US) most of his life.

Samidoh Muchoki mourns Mukundi

In a separate story, Samidoh, who knew Mukundi personally, shared a message of condolences.

Going on his Instagram Stories, Samidoh shared a short clip of him, Mukundi and other friends as they enjoyed a night out together.

Mukundi could be seen standing on Samidoh's right side in a black T-shirt as they listened to his acapella, with smiles on their faces.

The two shared a high five towards the end of the clip, with Mukundi in high spirits as they also shared selfies.

In his caption written in his Kikuyu dialect, Samidoh wondered what goes on in relationships that lead to such ordeals as he wished Mukundi to go in peace.
