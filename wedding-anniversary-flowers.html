Wedding Anniversary Flowers And Their Meanings

1

1st anniversary: Carnation

Serenata Flowers

Traditionally, the 1st wedding anniversary is celebrated with carnations. They symbolise admiration, whilst also being a sign of innocence.

2

2nd anniversary: Lily of the Valley

Serenata Flowers

These beautiful blooms are a sign of purity and have symbolised the devotion between two people for centuries. They're a wonderful way to celebrate the second year of marriage.

3

3rd anniversary: Sunflowers

Astronaut Images//Getty Images

The strong stems of a sunflower represent the strength of marriage. Sunflowers also turn their heads to follow the sun, which symbolises loyalty.

4

4th anniversary: Hydrangea

Serenata Flowers

Hydrangea symbolises the growing understanding between two people and is given on the fourth year of marriage. Plus, they look beautiful in any home.

5

5th anniversary: Daisy

ShotPrime//Getty Images

Meaning "forever love", daisies represent the surprises in marriage. While they might be a simple flower, they have beautiful intricate texture and petal structure.

6

6th anniversary: Calla

fotosr//Getty Images

The Calla Lily is an incredibly beautiful flower which symbolises growth. Sometimes often found in a stunning pink hue, it can also carry connotations of admiration and appreciation.

7

7th anniversary: Freesia

Serenata Flowers

For the 7th wedding anniversary, many give the gift of freesia: a bloom which represents trust and faithfulness.

8

8th anniversary: Lilac

Serenata Flowers

With its enchanting scent, captivating colours and heart-shaped leaves, lilacs symbolise first love.

9

9th anniversary: Bird of Paradise

Serenata Flowers

The 9th wedding anniversary is embodied in the Bird of Paradise flower – an exotic and magnificent flower symbolic of freedom and joy. This vibrant bloom has connotations of faithfulness and thoughtfulness.

10

10th anniversary: Daffodil

Beata Kontos//Getty Images

Daffodils are the celebration of a decade of marriage. It's a reminder of memories shared and the many exciting years still ahead.

11

25th anniversary: Iris

Pauline Lewis//Getty Images

Iris signifies faith, wisdom and promise. And what a beautiful way to celebrate 25 years of marriage.

12

50th anniversary: Yellow Rose and Violets

Ekaterina Smirnova//Getty Images

The celebration of 50 years truly does deserve something special. This is the only anniversary which is marked by two flowers: yellow roses and violets. Yellow roses are symbolic of beauty and prosperity whilst violets represent humility and commitment.
