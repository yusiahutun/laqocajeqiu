Best Celebrity Fashion Stylists To Follow On Instagram

 Posted on April 6, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  924 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Micaela Erlanger

Instagram

Who: @micaelaerlanger

Clients: Lupita Nyong'o, Meryl Streep, Naomie Harris, Tatiana Maslany, Jared Leto, Michelle Dockery, John Boyega

With a robust list of A-list clients (and Oscar winners) under her belt, Erlanger is best-known for turning Lupita Nyong'o into a red carpet style star. Most recently, she also took on Meryl Streep as a new client—making her one to keep a close eye on this award show season.

Karla Welch

Instagram

Erin Walsh

Instagram

Who: @erinwalshnyc

Clients: Sarah Jessica Parker, Kerry Washington, Maggie Gyllenhaal, Kristen Wiig

Known for her bold red carpet style—from SJP's Met Gala looks to Kerry Washington's maternity looks—Walsh styles some of the biggest red carpet risk takers in Hollywood. Her feed is filled with lots of shoes, rolling racks and A-Listers—just the kind of inspiration you'd want from a stylist.

Kate Young

Instagram

Who: @kateyoung

Clients: Natalie Portman, Selena Gomez, Margot Robbie, Sienna Miller, Michelle Williams

With an eclectic clientele that ranges from Selena Gomez to Natalie Portman, Young knows how to transform a star into a fashion It Girl. Take Selena Gomez, for example, whose style has completely transformed from Disney star to sexy, polished woman since starting to work with Young last year.

Cristina Ehrlich

Instagram

Who: @cristinaehrlich

Clients: Brie Larson, Penelope Cruz, Tina Fey, Gugu Mbatha-Raw, Uzo Aduba

Ehrlich has a penchant for turning fresh-faced actresses into full-blown red carpet stars (Margot Robbie, Brie Larson, etc). With a list of well-established style stars as clients, all eyes are on Ehrlich this season.

Ilaria Urbinati

Instagram

Who: @ilariaurbinati

Clients: Lizzy Caplan, Bradley Cooper, Laura Dern, Shailene Woodley, Nina Dobrev, Ryan Reynolds

Whether it comes to Hollywood's top leading actors or actresses, Urbinati has both men's and women's style on lock. The stylist can create red carpet magic in the form of an unexpected gown (case in point: Lizzy Caplan's Met Gala look) just as well as she can from a well-styled three-piece suit (ahem, Bradley Cooper).

Leslie Fremar

Instagram

Who: @lesliefremar

Clients: Julianne Moore, Reese Witherspoon, Jennifer Connolly, Charlize Theron

Fremar balances a clientele of A-List actresses who always manage to look the most sophisticated on the red carpet.

Elizabeth Stewart

Instagram

Who: @elizabethstewart1

Clients: Cate Blanchett, Julia Roberts, Viola Davis, January Jones, Amanda Seyfried, Jessica Chastain

Stewart has a roster of elegant A-List actresses from Cate Blanchett and Julia Roberts to Viola Davis and Jessica Chastain. When it comes to the red carpet, this stylist always has a show-stopping look ready to top a best-dressed list.

Petra Flannery

Instagram

Who: @petraflannery

Clients: Emma Stone, Zoe Saldana, Claire Danes, Daisy Ridley, Carey Mulligan, Gwen Stefani

Topping The Hollywood Reporter's annual list of power stylists for 2015, Petra Flannery is a fashion force to be reckoned with. The stylist, who manages a clientele full of A-Listers, always goes for a surprise look on the red carpet whether it's in the form of Emma Stone's Lanvin jumpsuit on the red carpet or Daisy Ridley's galatic-inspired Star Wars: The Force Awakens press tour style.

Brandon Maxwell

Instagram

Who: @brandonmaxwell

Client: Lady Gaga

The stylist and designer helped put the lady in Lady Gaga's new grown up, elegant style. Dressing Gaga for her incredible Oscars performance last year and along her jazz tour with Tony Bennett, his styling has the perfect balance of old eccentric Gaga combined with polished ladylike silhouettes.

Law Roach

Instagram

Who: @luxurylaw

Clients: Zendaya and Celine Dion

The superstylist and America's Next Top Model judge is behind Zendaya's daring red carpet style and most recently, Celine Dion's major style transformation.

Jill Lincoln and Jordan Johnson

Instagram

Who: @jillandjordan

Clients: Jennifer Lawrence, Sofia Vergara, Kiernan Shipka, Jennifer Garner

The styling duo who branched off on their own after leaving the Rachel Zoe studio now have a clientele of Sofia Vergara, J.Law and Kiernan Shipka. Most recently, Lincoln and Johnson were the force behind Vergara's gorgeous bridal look and Jennifer Lawrence's Hunger Games Mockinjay Part 2 press tour wardrobe.

Monica Rose

Instagram

Who: @monicarosestyle

Clients: Kendall and Kylie Jenner, Gigi Hadid, Chanel Iman, Khloe and Kourtney Kardashian

Monica Rose is not only the powerhouse behind pretty much the entire Kardashian-Jenner clan, she's also the one you can thank for Gigi Hadid and Kendall Jenner's always on-point model off-duty street style. Follow along to see who the well dressed A-Listers are wearing and for the stylist's own two young kids who are already lightyears more stylish than you.

Victoria Sekrier

Instagram

Who: @victoriasekrier

Client: Alicia Vikander

Last season, Vikander accepted her award for Best Supporting Actress in a show-stopping yellow gown that the Internet instantly compared to Beauty & The Beast's Belle. Since then, she's been the force behind the actress's award-winning style on the red carpet.

Joseph Cassell

Instagram

Who: @josephcassell1

Clients: Taylor Swift, Gigi Hadid

Cassell has been styling Taylor Swift for years now—helping to transform her look from country cutie to sexy 1989 popstar and master of crop tops. The stylist is also responsible for Gigi Hadid's red carpet style from last year's Oscars after party circuit to her sexy appearance at the MTV VMA's with Swift's Bad Blood squad.
