Who Is Jonathan Taylor Thomas? Bio & Wiki

Jonathan Taylor Thomas was born Jonathan Taylor Weiss on 8th September 1981 in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania, United States. He holds American nationality and belongs to Dutch and Portuguese ethnicity. He is the son of Claudine Gonsalves (mother), a personal manager and social worker, and Stephen Weiss (father), an industrial sales manager. He grew up with his brother named, Joel Thomas Weiss. His uncle Jeff Weiss is an American playwright, impresario, and actor. As of 2023, his age is 41.

Regarding his education, Weiss graduated with honors from Chaminade College Preparatory School in West Hills, California, in 2000. After graduation, he enrolled at Harvard University, where he studied philosophy and history. Jonathan also spent his third year abroad at St. Andrews University in Scotland. Later, he finished his studies with excellent grades from the Columbia University School of General Studies.

Jonathan Taylor Thomas’ Career

Jonathan Taylor Thomas started his career in an American animated cartoon series, The Adventures of Spot, in 1987. Later, he appeared as Macaulay in the sketch comedy series, In Living Color in 1991. He also starred with Taran Noah Smith and Patricia Richardson for 179 episodes of Home Improvement in 1993. Similarly, his other television works are The Simpsons, Smallville, etc.
