Kenyan food timetable for weight loss, Chart - 2

Monday

Monday is considered as a liquid day when it comes to meal plans. You should drink vegetable soup, Juice of unsweetened fruit and water. You have to stay strong at this time. You can consider this time as a natural detoxification period for your body.

Tuesday

On Tuesday you should have only fresh vegetables. Eat vegetables as much as you can eat. You can make different dishes with fresh vegetables. You can also make a salad with this. White cabbage is a vegetable which contains no calorie, so it is necessary to include it in your diet plan of Tuesday.

Wednesday

Wednesday is also considered as a liquid day like Monday. You should have only juice, water and light vegetable soup.

Thursday

Thursday is a fruit day for you. Except for grapes, bananas and persimmon you can eat any of your favourite fruits in any quantity. Friday is considered as a pure protein diet day. You should eat only those products which contain a large and quality amount of protein. Saturday

Again Saturday is a liquid day for you. In your breakfast, lunch and dinner you should consume just light drinks throughout the day.

On Sunday you should start your day by drinking a cup of unsweetened tea and then eat two boiled eggs. At the time of lunch, you should eat oranges or kiwis or any kind of fruits and chicken soup. At dinner time, you should have salads with fresh leafy vegetables and tomatoes.
