How Much Is Eve Hewson Net Worth?

Eve Hewson has an estimated net worth of $8 million from her career as an actress.

She made her on-screen debut in 2005 short Lost and Found with her sister, Jordan Hewson, despite her parents' objections.

She made her feature-length debut in The 27 Club (2008). Eve took part in an acting program at the New York Film Academy in the same year,2008. She appeared in the music video and accompanying short film for Irish band The Script‘s song For the First Time in 2010.

In the drama film, This Must Be The Place (2011), Eve appeared in the lead role as young punk Mary, but she is most known for playing Nurse Lucy Elkins in Steven Soderbergh’s TV series The Knick (2014 to 2015).

Eve has also appeared on Blood Ties (2013) as Yvonne and Enough Said (2013) as Tess. In 2014, she landed Lucy Elkins's role on "The Knick" as part of the main cast.

Hewson has acted alongside notable stars such as Frances McDormand, James Gandolfini, Clive Owen, and Sean Penn.

She has appeared as Carol Donovan in Steven Spielberg's Cold War movie Bridge of Spies in October 2015. Also, she starred in Robin Hood (2018), The Lumaniries (2020), and Behind Her Eyes (2021). 

Hewson was nominated for Irish Woman of the Year by Tatler in 2013 for her impressive body of work in the film industry.

Who Is Eve Hewson? Family Details

Eve Hewson is known as Memphis Eve Sunny Day Hewson. She was born on 7 July 1991 in Dublin, Ireland as the second daughter of her parents Ali Hewson and Bono (Paul David Hewson).

Her mother Ali was an activist whereas, her father Bono, was the lead singer of band U2. In the family, Eve has three siblings, an older sister, Jordan, and two younger brothers, Elijah and John.

She was educated at St. Andrew’s College in Dublin and earned her degree from New York University in May of 2013.

Concerning her physical appearance, Eve stands at the height of 5 feet and 1 inch (155 cm) and weighs around 54 kg (119 pounds) in weight.
