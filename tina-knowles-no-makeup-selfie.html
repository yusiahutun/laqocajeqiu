Tina Knowles Lawson Looks Flawless with No Makeup, Messy Hair In Early Morning Selfie

 Posted on June 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  215 words  |  Aldo Pusey

She woke up like this!

Tina Knowles Lawson proved she is always a natural beautym even with messy hair and no makeup. The 61-year-old took a page from her daughter Beyonce and posted a selfie on Tuesday looking fresh faced a gorgeous!

"'I woke up like this' my curls from yesterday are replaced with frizz! Yikes," she captioned the photo, referencing her daughter's lyrics in the song "Flawless."

Despite having just rolled out of bed, it's clear where Queen Bey gets her looks from!

Back in July, Tina opened up about feeling sexy and confident in her 60s after appearing on the cover of Ebony magazine's Sexy Forever issue.

"When I was asked to do the Sexy Forever 'Ebony' magazine cover, my first question was, 'Me really?'" she told the mag. "I had some reservations but as I thought of all the women who tell me daily that I have inspired them to live a wholehearted life, which includes your sexuality. I am proud to be 61 years old and to not be putting limitations on my lifestyle. We can still be sexy and vibrant, fashionable, classy and fly until the day we die!"
