World's richest celebrity kids with a one-year-old worth almost 1billion

 Posted on January 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  634 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Celebrities having huge bank accounts is no surprise – but some of their kids are already richer than many average Joes could ever dream of being.

The likes of Kim Kardashian, Rihanna and Kylie Jenner are billionaires in their own rights. And it appears they've made some smart decisions which has already benefited their young children.

New research has suggested that some celebrity offspring are already rich beyond their wildest dreams – and they haven't even had to do anything for it. Research from OutdoorToys has found that Princess Charlotte of Wales, eight, is the world's richest celebrity child, with an estimated worth of almost £4billion.

That's no surprise, considering they are literal royalty and heirs to the throne. But the real shock comes when we get down to the children of some A-list celebrities.

Topping the list is Rza Athelston Mayers, one years old, who is the son of singer and mogul Rihanna and rapper A$AP Rocky. The couple keep their son very private, and only revealed his name a year after he was born.

But thanks to his parents' savvy careers, the tot is thought to be worth an enormous £943.5million. Rihanna is worth £1.4b, according to Forbes, thanks to her hugely successful Fenty beauty range and Savage X Fenty lingerie line.

Meanwhile, A$AP Rocky, real name, Rakim Kayers, is thought to be worth $20m from his music. Their second child, Riot Rose Mayers, is also sure to be rolling in it despite being months old.

Next on the list is Beyonce and Jay Z's eldest child, Blue Ivy Carter. The 12-year-old is worth an estimated £567.9m and is a star in her own right thanks to her appearances alongside her mum.

Beauty mogul and reality star Kylie Jenner sees her daughter Stormi, five, on the list too. The youngster is thought to be worth almost half a billion.

Stormi's cousin North West, 10, daughter of Kim Kardashian and Kanye West, is trailing right behind her. North is becoming a bit of a social media sensation with her funny TikToks and outspoken appearances on her family's reality show, and is said to be worth £295m.

Former couple Gwen Stefani and Gavin Rossdale have two children on the list, with Apollo Bowie Flynn, nine, worth £106m, and Zuma, 15, worth £63m. Interestingly, eldest child Kingston, 17, didn't make the list, but is believed to be worth $35m.

Jennifer Lopez's son with Marc Anthony, Max Muñiz, 16, comes next with £31m. His twin sister Emme, is believed to be worth $1m.

Valtnina Paloma Pinault, 16, is the daughter of actress Salma Hayek. She has been pictured with Salma at a number of events, and it said to be worth just under £10m.

Returning to the Kardashians, Khloe's daughter True Thompson, five, is said to be worth £7.8m. Surprisingly, none of Kourtney Kardashian's children were included in the list.

Rounding out the top 10 is Aubrey Frances Anderson-Emmons, with a respectable £4.7m. Unlike the others on the list, though, the 16-year-old is famous in her own right as an actress, and is best known for playing Lily Tucker-Pritchett in Modern Family.
