Dorothy Wang gets in nasty spat with Bianca Espada on Rich Kids Of Beverly Hills

 Posted on September 19, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  876 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Dorothy Wang isn’t jealous of anyone, especially not Rich Kids Of Beverly Hills newbie Bianca Espada.

The privileged pair got into a fight on Sunday night’s episode during Dorothy’s lavish birthday celebrations in Las Vegas.

Dorothy, a 28-year-old multi millionaire, had flown the whole crew to Sin City on her private plane to party.

Birthday drama: Dorothy Wang couldn't escape the drama at her own birthday bash on Sunday's episode of Rick Kids Of Beverly Hills

Bianca brought her own security guard to the event, then accused Dorothy of being jealous of her.

‘The people that actually are something don’t act like they are,’ said Jonny Drubel imperiously.

‘It’s my birthday, why are you making this about you?’ asked Dorothy, who seemed genuinely bemused by the row that was erupting.

‘I’m with a bunch of haters,’ said Bianca, before storming out in tears.

Own security: Bianca brought her own security guard to the birthday bash 

Not mad: Dorothy insisted she wasn't mad about the security guard but the drama still escalated

‘She thinks I’m jealous of her? B**** has lost her mind,’ laughed Dorothy, but she later felt bad and tried to draw Bianca out of her hotel room with a cocktail.

Bianca however refused to open the door.

‘I offered her the olive branch,’ frowned Dorothy who left the drink outside her door.

Making up: The birthday girl brought Bianca a drink but she refused to open the door

Behind the door: Bianca refused to open the door to see Dorothy

Parting gift: Dorothy left the drink on the floor for Bianca

As the crew argued Brendan Fitzpatrick decided to elope with Morgan Stewart at the chapel down the road.

‘We’re drunk enough, my hair is in a pony, why not just get it done?’ agreed Morgan.

They were at the alter when Brendan called it off.

Wedding chapel: Morgan and Brendan were pretty close to getting married at a Vegas chapel

‘I don’t think this is the place,’ he decided.

‘I was on a cocktail of over-the-counter medication that caused my decision making to be a little bit fuzzy,’ Morgan decided later.

‘I can’t believe we even made it to the chapel,’ she marveled as they nursed their hangovers the next day.

Not the place: Brendan at the last second decided the chapel wasn't the right place to get married

The posse flew out without Bianca who was still offended.

‘Another eventful birthday,’ sighed Dorothy.

Morgan earlier had been furious at Bianca for calling her 150 person wedding a ‘cocktail party’.

‘I’m Mexican and we throw weddings for 600 people and we serve breakfast. To me it’s a little small, but it’s your wedding, your choice,’ shrugged Bianca when Morgan confronted her.

Face to face: Morgan and Biana met to air their differences face to face

Bianca then called Morgan out for calling Dorothy ‘**** Twinkie’ behind her back.

‘It’s ugly for me to say it…I feel so badly about it,’ Morgan backtracked later of the Asian slur.

Meanwhile, EJ Johnson, the 23-year-old son of basketball great Magic Johnson, had a manicure and gossip with his friends Kyle and Drew in New York City.

Famous father: EJ Johnson who is the son of Earvin 'Magic' Johnson went to New York City with friends

EJ named his green nail varnish ‘Jaded’ and discussed options for the line.

The openly gay fashionista then met with some LGBT officials for an art viewing.

They looked at a bathroom mural designed by Keith Haring.

‘This is my kind of artwork,’ he cooed as they admired the phallic drawings.

Bathroom mural: A bathroom mural by Keith Haring was admired by EJ

EJ was so moved by the work of the LGBT support group that he decided to help.

‘I’m going to start by giving the proceeds of the nail polish to the center,’ he said.

Morgan and Brendan saw an astrologist to talk about their pending wedding date.

Wedding planning: Brendan and Morgan saw an astrologer about their wedding

Bad date: Susan Miller of Astrology Zone warned the couple about their May 7 wedding date

‘It’s really not an ideal date,’ astrologer Susan Miller told them, advising that they secretly get married ‘a month or two ahead’ to bypass the bad omens connected to May 7.

Morgan then fretted that they both needed to do ‘a cleanse’ and a ‘series of facials’ with lasers.

‘You need to look better than you ever have looked in your life,’ she told Brendan.

Cleanse time: Morgan told Brendan they both were going to do a cleanse before their wedding

Dorothy and Jonny met for dinner, but Dorothy was on her own cleanse.

‘I feel that it is appropriate for Dorothy to go on a cleanse…her face and neck were sharing a room for a while there,’ giggled Morgan.

The Rich Kids Of Beverly Hills continues next week on Bravo.
