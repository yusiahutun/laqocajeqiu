Michael Keaton, 68, takes a break from isolation as he enjoys an energetic jog around his local area

 Posted on April 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  423 words  |  Larita Shotwell

He's joined his country in self-isolation in a bid to stem the spread of coronavirus. 

And Michael Keaton took a break from isolation as he enjoyed an energetic jog around his neighbourhood in Brentwood, California on Wednesday.

The 68-year-old actor appeared in good spirits as he pounded the pavement as he kept himself in shape.

Fitness: Michael Keaton took a break from isolation as he enjoyed an energetic jog around his neighbourhood in Brentwood, California on Wednesday

The Dumbo star was dressed causally for his outing, donning a grey hoodie worn over a plain white T-shirt. 

He added dark grey jogging bottoms and navy trainers to his look, adding a yellow sports cap and dark sunglasses.
