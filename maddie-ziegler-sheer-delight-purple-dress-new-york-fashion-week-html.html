Maddie Ziegler is a sheer delight in see-through purple dress while out during New York Fashion Week

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  720 words  |  Martina Birk

Maddie Ziegler was spotted on the streets of Nomad on Monday as she made her way around New York Fashion Week. 

The 20-year-old Dance Moms alum wore a sleeveless purple dress under a cream skirt for her second look of the day.

Ziegler styled her long, dark tresses straight with a center part and clutched a light blue handbag as she crossed the street between presentations.

Draped over her shoulders, she wore a shiny white long trench coat with rolled sleeves which she teamed with a pair of cream heels with silver toes.

Her eyelids were dusted in a shimmery golden brown eyeshadow, revealing her blue eyes.

The media personality added dark mascara that extended her fluttery lashes as she walked between the star-studded Fashion Week events.

Maddie's cosmetics look consisted of lightly bronzed cheeks and matte brown lipstick.

Earlier Monday, Maddie wore a floral dress alongside actress Melissa Barrera at the Carolina Herrera show. 

The beauties posed in their floral frocks at the fashion show at The Plaza Hotel in New York City.

NYFW, which officially began earlier Friday, is known as being part of the 'Big Four,' as well as London, Milan, and also Paris Fashion Week.

Maddie, who has starred in several of Sia's music videos, including Chandelier and Elastic Heart, is best known for her role on the reality series Dance Moms.

She gained worldwide fame thanks to Sia's videos, which made the singer protective of her.

'I do check in with Maddie weekly about whether she wants this, and assure her if she ever wants it to stop, it stops,' the Unstoppable singer tweeted.

Pittsburgh native Maddie appeared in HBOs The Fallout and Steven Spielberg's West Side Story.

Last year, Maddie attended the 94th Annual Academy Awards in support of West Side Story which was nominated for seven Oscars, including Best Picture, Best Supporting Actress, and Best Director. 

Ariana DeBose from West Side Story won for Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role.

Maddie also stars as Mia Reed in The Fallout, a 2021 American teen drama film about a high school student trying to navigate her trauma following a school shooting.

The beauty opened up about her transition from dancer to actor in a January interview with Collider. 

'When I was younger, I was just like, "Oh yeah, dance til I die. That’s it. That’s my path forever."

‘For some reason, that was all I could think of until I started doing music videos with Sia,’ said Maddie.

Dance Moms followed the training and careers of children in dance and show business under the tutelage of Abby, now 56, and the relationships between the instructor, dancers, and their bickering mothers.

As a child, the rising star was featured on the first six seasons of the Lifetime reality show from 2011 to 2016. 

Her dance instructor Abby infamously took the news of Maddie leaving - delivered by her mom Melissa - very hard as she locked herself in a room.
