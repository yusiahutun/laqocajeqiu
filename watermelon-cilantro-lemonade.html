Watermelon-Cilantro Lemonade

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  124 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Yields: 8

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 1 c.

    fresh lemon juice

  • 1 c.

    sugar

  • 1 c.

    chopped seedless watermelon, plus wedges for garnish

  • 1/2 c.

    fresh cilantro, plus sprigs for garnish

Directions

  • Step 1Combine lemon juice, sugar, watermelon, and cilantro in a mason jar. Muddle with a muddler or the handle of a wooden spoon until watermelon is broken down. Tightly screw on the lid, and shake until the sugar is dissolved, about 1 minute. Let sit 1 hour.
  • Step 2Pour sugar mixture into a large pitcher. Top with 6 cups water and ice. Serve over ice garnished watermelon wedges and cilantro sprigs.
