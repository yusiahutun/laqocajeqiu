Daphne Zuniga began her acting journey with the '80s horror movie called The Dorm That Dripped Blood. But, it was her role in American Dreams and Melrose Place, which earned her wide recognition from the audience. 

In addition to her acting credits, Daphne has worked as a producer and director in TED: The Future We Will Create. 

Wedding Details, Husband Info

Daphne got married to her long-time boyfriend, David Mleczko, on 8 June 2019. The pair became husband and wife in front of 30 close family members and friends. The actress dazzled in her Nicole Miller gown while her man complemented Daphne in a dapper suit.

Family Bio & Ethnicity

Daphne was born to her parents: mother Agnes A. Zuniga (née Janawicz) and father Joaquin Alberto Zuniga Mazariegos on 28 October 1962. The 56-year-old boasts mixed ethnicity consisting of Guatemalan-Polish-Finnish descent. 

Sharing the ethnicity are her sisters; Jennifer Zuniga and Rosario Zuniga. Interestingly, Daphne’s siblings have also divulged into acting. Jennifer worked alongside her sister Daphne in Melrose Place, while Rosario has acting credits in Salvador, Lolo, and A Silent Love.

Daphne's Net Worth 

Daphne has been working consistently in Hollywood since her debut in the '80s. Boasting over 70 acting credits to date, the veteran actress has a net worth of $3 million. 

Her notable projects include Models Inc., The Outer Limits, Stone Pillow, Christmas Do-Over, and Mail Order Bride. Further, the University of California, Los Angeles alum is also recognized for her work in the TV show, One Tree Hill. 

And, she is not slowing down anytime soon. Daphne’s untitled Christmas project is under production as of 2019.

With her paycheck from acting, Daphne invested in real estate. In 2003, she sold her Los Angeles home for $1.63 million. Previously, the actress of height 5 feet 8 inches (1.73 m) had sold her other LA house in 2001 for $490K.
