The first warm breezes of spring have finally arrived and summer feels like it’s just around the next corner. While it may not be full-on swimsuit season yet,  there are plenty of hot tub moments and tropical spring break getaways to enjoy while the weather is still a little chilly—and I know exactly where you can find the cutest swim styles to rock this season. I’ve rounded up a few of my personal favorite places to buy swimsuits online, no matter your budget. So go ahead, save and buy in bulk or splurge a little!

Spring might’ve just started, but you can never go wrong with stocking up on a few new bathing suits ahead of summer. I love my basic black string bikini as much as the next girl, but buying something new sparks some serious joy. And we’ll take all the happy moments we can get these days.

Shopping for a new bikini or one-piece can seem daunting, I know, but you’re basically guaranteed to find your new favorite swimsuit from one of these sites. If you’re trying to pick up a wear-everywhere option that won’t break the bank, consider shopping on ASOS or PrettyLittleThing. If you’d rather splurge on something special, why not browse Net-A-Porter or Revolve’s wide selections? There are also a ton of smaller brands to consider that your friends may not already be shopping, from L*Space to Kitty and Vibe to Summersalt and more.

Let’s dive in, shall we? Keep reading to scope out the best sites for swimwear on the web. We’ll keep updating this story as we test more and more swimsuits!

How We Chose the Best Swimsuits on the Internet

These choices are a mix of personal reviews from the StyleCaster editorial team, public reviews posted on brand websites, and editor conferrals. Every brand and product we chose we feel confident is high-quality and worth the price tag — and your time!

  • Target

    Shade & Shore Longline Cut Out Bikini Top

    BRB—I’m on my way to buy literally everything from Target right now.  Their swimwear range includes tons of affordable brands like Kona Sol, Aqua Green and Shade & Shore. This cute two-piece is equally comfy and stylish.

    Shipping and return policy: free shipping and returns.

    Pros

    • Fashionable styles
    • Easy shipping and returns

    Cons

    • Sizes and styles sell out very quickly

  • Anthropologie

    LSPACE Jess Bikini Top

    We can always depend on Anthropologie for truly unique homeware, clothes and swimsuits. The brand is constantly adding new styles to its ever-growing collection, so there’s never an issue finding something that’s brand new to your wardrobe and to the market. This high-waisted bikini set has the perfect amount of coverage.

    Shipping and returns: Free shipping on orders $50+, free returns in-store within 30 days

    Pros

    • Can return easily in stores

    Cons

    • Can be expensive

  • Summersalt

    The Perfect Wrap One-Piece

    How cute is this color-block one-piece from Summersalt? The female-founded brand got famous thanks to their widely-flattering one-pieces and selection of bathing suits designed for those of us with longer torsos. I’ve been a fan of bandeau-style bikinis for a few years now, but this option is taking my love to a whole new level. Style this top with the high-waisted bottoms for a truly retro feel.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping with purchases over $200 and free exchanges/returns within 20 days

    Pros

    • Great size range
    • Multiple colors and patterns in each style

    Cons

    • Most styles are hand wash only

  • Nordstrom

    La Blanca Lace-Up Underwire One-Piece Swimsuit

    Belted one-piece swimsuits are my new favorite thing and Nordstrom has tons right now. Shopping on the mass retailer’s site should be a no-brainer when it comes to swimwear—they have lots of brands at a variety of different price points to choose from. This one-piece from La Blanca is flirty and has the cutest details.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping and returns

    Pros

    • Nordstrom displays styling options for each item
    • Variety
    • Seamless shopping experience

    Cons

    • Inconsistent size guides
    • They don’t always carry your favorite brands

  • Venus

    Bora Bora Bikini Bottom

    Venus carries a full line of accessories and clothing, but I’m here gush specifically about their swimwear. They carry brands like Sports Illustrated as well as their own (massive) in-house range.

    Shipping and returns: $7.95 shipping, $6.95 returns

    Pros

    • Multiple coverage options for each style

    Cons

    • $6.95 fee for returns

  • Lane Bryant

    Faux-Wrap Swim Skirt

    On Sale 70% off

    Lane Bryant is known for their elegant selection of plus-size sleepwear, loungewear and lingerie, but their swimwear line is so, so good, too. This wrap-inspired bikini has the cutest print and comes in sizes 10 through 32.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping on orders $125+, returns may incur a fee

    Pros

    • Great size range

    Cons

    • Cannot exchange items, must place new order and return

  • Andie Swim

    The Mykonos One Piece

    Andie has a wide range of styles, but the brand even has a page for long-torso swimsuits. The Mykonos one-piece features stunning contrasting binding and straps. The black, light blue and orange color options are so elegant that you simply can’t go wrong with any of them.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping on orders $150+, free exchanges

    Pros

    • Removable cups

    Cons

    • Hand wash only

  • Adore Me

    Shelby Contour Plus Bikini

    On Sale 50% off

    It’s no secret that Adore Me has one of the best size ranges on the web when it comes to underwear and lingerie, but you need to check out their swimsuits too! This floral balconette-style bikini is totally retro and comes in sizes up to a 4X.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping and exchanges

    Pros

    • Inclusive size range
    • Comes as a set
    • Can choose different size top and bottom

    Cons

    • Taller reviewers noted swim bottoms weren’t “high waisted” as described

  • Aerie

    Crinkle One-Piece Swimsuit

    On Sale 25% off

    Yes, Aerie makes killer workout gear and has some of the best underwear selections on the market, but do not overlook their range of swimwear. It’s just as flattering (and affordable!) as you’d hope it would be. The best part about this particular cool blue suit? The new crinkle fabric is made for maximum stretch so you get a contoured, customized fit to your body whenever you put it on.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping with orders $75+, free returns

    Pros

    • Can easily return products to American Eagle/Aerie stores
    • Frequent sales

    Cons

    • Styles sell out quickly

  • Frankies Bikinis

    Cola Underwire Balconette Bikini Top

    Frankies Bikinis knows how to make a good swimsuit. Whether they’re collaborating with the likes of Gigi Hadid or just showing off their styles on Instagram, I always leave room for a new suit from them in my collection. Case in point: this gorgeous floral and red set. I love triangle bikini tops because they’re super sexy. Pair this colorful top with the coordinating bottoms for an extra-vibrant look.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping and free returns

    Pros

    • Cute, trendy styles
    • Free shipping and returns

    Cons

    • High price points

  • Kitty and Vibe

    Ruffle String Top

    On Sale 20% off

    Every swimsuit bottom from Kitty and Vibe is designed with a new sizing metric that takes more into account than just your waist. There are actually different options depending on how big your booty is. Because of this, this ruffled bikini checks all the right boxes. It’s time to say goodbye to ill-fitting swimsuits in 2024.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping on orders $75+, free exchanges

    Pros

    • Frequent sales
    • Inclusive size range

    Cons

    • Reviewers with a larger bust noted that fit was difficult

  • Blackbough

    Alana Bandeau Top

    I just got this bikini and I’m absolutely in love. My favorite thing about Blackbough is that they’re constantly dropping new styles that are unique, cute, and have special details like the bottom shown here; one side is tie up and the other is stretch.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping on orders over $125, return for exchange or store credit only

    Pros

    • New, unique styles

    Cons

    • Smaller coverage on bottoms

  • Gooseberry

    One Piece Cutout Black

    Gooseberry is known for its intimates collection, but it also recently expanded into swim, offering chic one-pieces, bikinis, and more. We love this one-piece with a cutout that makes it look like you’re vacationing in Italy, even when you’re just poolside in Jersey.

    Shipping and returns: free shipping on orders over $125, returns for exchange or store credit

    Pros

    • Multiple colors available in each style

    Cons

    • Can only return for store credit/exchange

  • Montce

    Gold Filigree Simone Bikini Top

    Montce has a stunning assortment of swimwear, athleisure, accessories, and even a mommy and me line. I’m particularly obsessed with their swim line. This golden filigree style is so chic and if you’re a fan of white bikinis, a little pattern is a great way to break up the total white space.

    Shipping and returns: Free shipping on orders over $100, free exchanges

    Pros

    • Unique patterns and designs

    Cons

    • Higher price point
