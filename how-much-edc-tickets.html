The Electric Daisy Carnival is just weeks away, taking place on May 17, 2024 through May 19, 2024 at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway. Diplo, David Guetta, DJ Snake, Dillon Francis and John Summit are some of the biggest names performing, but attendees can also expect to see Zedd, Deadmau5, Kaskade and more take the stage.

With the festival on the horizon, EDC three-day GA passes are already sold out (and VIP and individual day passes are selling fast) on the festival’s website. But if you’re still on the hunt for last minute tickets, there are options available to ensure you don’t miss seeing all your favorite DJ’s sets live.

EDC Las Vegas hails from the Los Angeles-based Insomniac Events, and is the largest electronic dance music festival in North America. Each years invited artists incorporate a variety of electronic music into their performances, including house, techno, drum and bass, and dubstep. Attendees can catch all of the live music acros nine different stages, kineticFIELD, cosmicMEADOW, circuitGROUNDS, neonGARDEN, bassPOD, wasteLAND, quantumVALLEY, stereoBLOOM and bionicJUNGLE.

So how can EDM music fans still get access to EDC tickets in 2024? Keep scrolling for everything you need to know about snagging tickets on resale sites, headliners, performers, and more—including a discount code to save on your passes.

How much are EDC tickets?

How much are EDC 2024 tickets? As of right now, three-day GA tickets to the festival are sold out on EDC’s website. If you’re still trying to find a ticket pass, three-day passes and single-day tickets are still available on a variety of ticket reseller sites, like StubHub, TicketNetwork, TicketSmarter, Ticketmaster, and Vivid Seats, the latter of which offers $20 off of orders of $200 or more with the code SC2022—a 10 percent savings.

While resale passes usually end up costing you more than the original ticket release on the festival’s website, this is your best option for scoring passes in the weeks leading up to the festival. Resale prices for EDC currently range from $414 to $629 for a GA three-day pass, with Stubhub currently offering the most affordable prices.

    Who are the EDC 2024 headliners?

    Who are the EDC 2024 headliners? The EDC 2024 festival has pulled some big names for this year’s line-up, including John Summit, Martin Garrix, Armin van Buuren, David Guetta, Peggy Gou, Tiesto, Diplo and Steve Aoki. Individual set times have yet to be released.

    Where is EDC 2024?

    Where is EDC 2024? The Electronic Daisy Carnival has been held at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway in Las Vegas, Nevada, since 2011. The venue is located at 7000 N. Las Vegas Blvd. Las Vegas, NV 89115, and is 20 minute drive from the Las Vegas Strip and a 30 minute drive from the Harry Reid International Airport.
