Kehlani began her music career at a young age.

Kehlani Ashley Parrish, known professionally as Kehlani, was born on April 24, 1995 in Oakland, California. She started singing and dancing as a child, performing in talent shows and local events.

She gained recognition on the reality show “America’s Got Talent”.

At the age of 14, Kehlani auditioned for the sixth season of “America’s Got Talent” and made it to the final 25 contestants. Although she didn’t win, her talent and determination were noticed by industry professionals.

Kehlani’s breakthrough came with her mixtape “Cloud 19”.

Released in 2014, “Cloud 19” showcased Kehlani’s soulful R&B sound and gained significant attention from music critics and fans alike. The mixtape features her popular track “FWU” which helped establish her as a rising star in the music industry.

She has collaborated with renowned artists.

Kehlani has worked with notable artists such as Chance the Rapper, Zayn Malik, G-Eazy, and Cardi B. These collaborations have helped her expand her musical reach and showcase her versatility as an artist.

Kehlani’s debut studio album, “SweetSexySavage”, was a commercial success.

Released in 2017, “SweetSexySavage” debuted at number three on the Billboard 200 chart and spawned hit singles like “CRZY” and “Distraction”. The album received positive reviews for its honest and empowering lyrics.

She is known for her honest and personal songwriting.

Kehlani is admired for her ability to express vulnerability and share personal experiences through her music. Her lyrics tackle various topics such as love, heartbreak, mental health, and self-empowerment.
