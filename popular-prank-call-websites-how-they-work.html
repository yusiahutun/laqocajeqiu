8 Popular Prank Call Websites and How They Work

 Posted on April 3, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1292 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

A good and safe prank call never harmed anybody. In fact, it can be a light and funny escape from our monotonous routines, and bring spontaneity into our lives. If you nod in agreement then you'll love some of these top prank call websites that you can trick your friends with.

We've scoured the internet and have come up with some of the best options out there. So, let's dive right in.

1. Prank Dial

On top of our list is Prank Dial, one of the best prank call websites on the internet. The website has a smooth and interactive interface with a vast category of different prank call scenarios to choose from. Categories vary from technology, scams, funny, politics, celebrity-themed phone calls, and more.

For instance, if giving a good scare to one of your buddies is what you are after, then you can use the Why Would You Call My Girlfriend prank. But if that's not your style, then don't lose hope, as the site is filled with plenty of other cheeky and funny prank calls.

Just pick a scenario and go ahead with it. Your recipient will hear this call recording over their phone. To make sure they don't panic from the call, you can add a pre-recorded message at the end of the prank, which reveals to your friends that they just got pranked.

2. Comedy Calls

Another free prank call website that deserves to be on this list is Comedy Calls. Similar to Prank Dial, it has different categories of calls you can choose to prank from.

There's the classic RickRoll prank call that will do exactly what its name implies—RickRoll your friends. And if you're looking for more ways to annoy your friends, then you'll love the Phone Pranks section.

Since the first call is free, you can try it and see if you like the service. If you like it, you can conduct some more prank calls by purchasing credits from the site.

3. Spoof Box

Want to annoy your friends with a prank call from a Karen? For those unaware of the eccentric cultural stereotype, a "Karen" is an entitled middle-aged woman that always makes a scene when things don't go her way. If this is your thing, then The Entitled Lady scenario from Spoof Box is what you're looking for.

And unlike the previous prank call websites, Spoof Box also offers an interactive mode that lets you speak with your target. And if pranking a single person isn't enough, then there's also a feature to do group prank calls.

It also has a feature to spoof your phone number with a fake number. These features make Spoof Box a unique option that you can use to make funny prank calls.

4. Prank Hotline

Prank Hotline is another popular, fake call website. With Prank Hotline, you get three free prank calls. After that, you'll have to pay for the services.

There are a host of pranks to choose from. Select the one that suits your fancy and click on the Send This Prank option. On the next screen, enter the contact number of your recipient. Optionally, you can also enter a fake number that you'd like to send the call from. Select the Send This Prank Call button to go ahead with the call.

Moreover, the website offers a lot of exciting premium features and extra calls that you can buy using the site's tokens. However, for that, you'll first have to create an account on the website.

5. Genie Calls

Gene Calls is another popular prank call site. Again, like Spoof Box, you can do spoof calls, as well as group prank calls. Upon arriving at the homepage, you'll see a variety of call recordings to select from.

When you find the one that you'd like to use, click on the small Call icon on the scenario's thumbnail. In the next window, you can choose a simple prank call, a group call, or a spoof call where you mask your real number with a fake one.

6. Blow Up the Phone

Blow Up the Phone is slightly different from our fake call websites that we've covered so far in that instead of calls, the website will flood your target with a tsunami of text messages. The text, of course, will be sent from multiple random numbers. Presently, it's only available in the US and Canada.

To get started, select your target's country of residence, enter the contact number and click on Prank This Number. On the next page, select the number of messages and their frequency. The texts will be totally random and won't make any sense. This makes it, we believe, a great compliment to top Android apps for pranking your friends.

7. Spoof My Phone

Another popular prank call website on the internet, Spoof My Phone, will spoof your number for the prank call. You can choose any phone number that you'd like to use, and successfully disguise yourself using this service.

Just like the previous sites, Spoof My Phone has tons of options like sound effects, group call spoofing, and more. But unlike other sites, it offers two additional features: a recording option and a voice changer.

With the recording option, you can record whatever you want to say to your recipient beforehand, and then send that recording as a call, instead of choosing a random pre-recorded scenario.

The second feature is the voice changer. So, when you are recording your call, you can use the voice changer to alter your voice, masking your identity even further. There's also an option that you can use to change your voice to a female or male.

To earn a free prank call, you'll have to like and share the site's Twitter or Facebook page. If you aren't comfortable with that for whatever reason, you can pay for a prank call through the credits it offers. You can pay for the credits with PayPal or Bitcoin.

8. Prank Owl

Want to make two friends call each other and record the call while you’re at it? If so, you should give Prank Owl a go.

By default, you’ll get two free tokens for the first time you use the service. A call of 30 seconds will cost you a single token. So, all in all, you get a full minute to online prank call anyone for free.

But this isn’t all, as the website offers a slew of other free prank call variations as well. There is, for instance, a prank called ‘Cat Facts!’, which throws a random barrage of facts about cats to your target listener.

Another hilarious prank is that of ‘Radio Contest’. You know those weird calls where a host announces you’ve won huge, exciting prizes out of the blue? This is that prank. Call up your friends and surprise them with random contest wins pranks.

However, this is not to be confused with those scam calls that ask you for your personal information. In fact, we've written a complete guide on identifying fake calls, which you should check out and educate yourself with.

The only downside we found was the categorization of pranks into free and premium, as this limits your access to free pranks. To access other cool pranks, you'll need to get premium access to the website.

The Best Free Prank Websites for Tricking Your Friends

Hopefully, you found the free prank call website that you feel is the most hilarious. Although the internet sometimes gets a bad rep for a variety of reasons, it does have its light corners like these, which makes it a truly fun place.
