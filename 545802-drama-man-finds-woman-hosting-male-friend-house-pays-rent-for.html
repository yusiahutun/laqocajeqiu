Kenyans Amused by Video of Man Finding Woman Hosting Male Friend in House He Pays Rent

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  397 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Neighbours in a Nairobi apartment were treated to a scene when a man stormed one of the houses and pulled out a visitor
  • It emerged that the youngster had visited a woman in a house whose monthly rent was paid by the older man
  • In a video that went viral on social media, the young man was captured pleading for leniency as he was furiously dragged around

A man was furious when he discovered a woman he had been financially supporting hosting a male friend in the house he rents for her.

The incident, which occurred in an unnamed residential neighbourhood, left the young man embarrassed to the core.

Neighbours gathered to witness scene unfold

A viral video shared on Facebook by Lemiso Media House captured the moment the man barged into the house.

Evidently, the young man had not been informed of the visitor and had believed the woman occupied the residence alone.

Before they could fathom what was happening, he had been unceremoniously pulled out of the house.

The noise and screams from the commotion attracted the attention of the neighbours, who quickly gathered around to savour the moment.

Social media reactions

While the details of the sag remain unknown, it was clear the man felt betrayed and questioned the nature of the woman’s relationship with her guest.

The incident has sparked discussions among residents and on social media platforms, with many advising men against visiting women's houses.
