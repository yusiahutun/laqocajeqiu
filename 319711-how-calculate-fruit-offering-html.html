Different offerings are an essential requirement at worship places as commanded in divine writings. First fruits is one of the most familiar forms of offering taught continually among Christians and other groups of believers. It is therefore important for all believers to understand how to calculate first fruit offering so that they can do it right and get the blessings attached to it.

According to the Bible, it is of great importance to give first fruits offering as part of your worship. Every believer, therefore, needs a thorough understanding of this traditional observance regarding how and when it is given. The Hebrew, Greek, and Roman founded faiths regarded first fruits as a priestly offering of the deity. The term has seen drastic development over the years and is now referred to as donations or more precise, offering in contemporary Christian faiths. What is a first fruit offering?

A mention of first fruit offering is rampant in the Bible where it is observed among worshippers for its traditional and present-day significance. Simply put, first fruits refer to a religious offering taken from the harvest of the first agricultural produce. The ancient church dedicated the same to the priesthood. Christians, on the other hand, receive first fruits in the form of the tithe with a lesser sense of autonomy. By that, it means first fruits are based on personal commitment while tithes are ten percent offering of all incomes, commissions, bonuses, and paychecks received.

Firstfruits were initially meant for the sustenance of religious facilities and leaders. Some Christians regard Jesus as the first fruit having risen from the dead. Why do we give a first fruit offering?

Dedicating our first fruits to God is a reminder enough that God is of a higher priority in our lives. It is also a demonstration that we are continually grateful for God’s provision. Let us now explore in depths what the Bible says about first fruit offering.

What percentage is the first fruit offering?

Unlike tithing, it is not straightforward determining how to calculate first fruit offering because first fruit offering scriptures provide no particular measure concerning this type of donation. While some people argue that first fruits are a down payment for God’s due richness and blessing, others hold that it is a confirmation that God has already blessed humanity. It is on this understanding that first fruits should be chosen from the richness abounded in the harvest as opposed to leftovers.

Understanding first fruits offering - the Biblical perspective

The Bible points out precisely how everyone should dedicate their tithes and first fruit offering in their worship. The books of law in the Bible point out precisely how, when, and why one should dedicate their first fruits for the service of the lord.

How do you calculate the first fruit offering?

People have different measures of firstfruits hence, variations in their calculations. In essence, first fruits are not systematic therefore no standard mathematical approach is acceptable. In practice, fruit offering is given thrice in a year; in January to mark a good start of the year, June for celebrating God’s continued care, and December for thanksgiving to God for his yearlong protection.
