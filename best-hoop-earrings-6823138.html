The 15 Best Hoop Earrings of 2024

 Posted on July 2, 2024  |  14 minutes  |  2903 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Hoop earrings have stood the very tough test of time. Just think: everyone from Twiggy and Cher to Jennifer Lopez and Rihanna have worn them, and they even donned the lobes of a few boy band members you probably crushed on back in the day too. Decade after decade, hoop earrings look equally fresh and timeless, whether it’s laundry day or your wedding day. There’s no going wrong with a classic, classy hoop earring. 

As we shopped for the best hoop earrings, each of our selections had to check off three boxes to make the cut: they had to be made from quality materials, with a long-lasting style, and a certain je ne sais quoi that’ll turn heads. Whether you’re shopping for a cuff-like huggie for your cartilage, an oversized pair for special occasions, or something in between, we’ve narrowed down our list to 10 picks we love and think you will too.

At the top of that list are Jennifer Fisher 2’’ Thread Hoops, which checks all those requirements and more. (They’re also lightweight and affordable too.) The others that made our list vary in style and price points, but they all would instantly elevate any outfit with little effort required. Buy them now, and wear them forever.

What to Keep in Mind

Material

If you’re at all sensitive to metals, pay close attention to the material before committing to a pair of hoop earrings. It’s best to steer clear of nickel, which is the most common metal allergy and can cause pain, itching, and swelling. Instead, opt for gold or platinum and stainless steel, which are the best hypoallergenic metals, when choosing earrings for sensitive ears. If you prefer gold earrings, the higher the karat, the purer the metal — and the more likely you are to be able to wear them all day without your earlobes aching. 

Size

Hoops come in one shape, but many sizes. Before adding any pair of hoop earrings to your online cart, determine if the hoops’ dimensions will fit into your lifestyle and style in general. Small, lightweight hoops can be thrown on every day, with nearly any outfit, while huggies best-fit cartilage piercings. Oversized pairs act as showstoppers but probably aren’t practical if you have a grabby-hand baby or long, textured hair that tends to get tangled in them.  

Your Questions, Answered

What’s the difference between gold and gold-plated jewelry?

Gold is exactly what it sounds like: a solid piece of the precious metal, often found in 10K or 14K options. While it’s the highest quality of gold jewelry options, it’s also the most expensive. If your budget doesn’t allow for a pair of solid gold hoop earrings, opt for gold-plated instead. Gold-plated jewelry is created with a thin layer of gold applied atop another base metal, like brass, copper, or silver. No matter which option you choose, all gold tarnishes in time. With proper maintenance though (more on that below), either option should last you for years to come. 

How do you clean hoop earrings?

Between body oil, makeup, sunscreen, and hairspray, earrings can quickly accumulate unwanted build-up. To restore them to their best, shiny selves, aim to clean your earrings once a month. If that sounds daunting, it’s not. Cleaning hoop earrings is ultra-easy. 

Precious metals like gold and silver are soft, so they simply need to be gently wiped down with a dry microfiber or polishing cloth. For more thorough cleaning, mix two tablespoons of mild dish soap with one quart of warm water. Soak your jewelry in that mixture for 10 to 20 minutes, then gently rinse and pat dry with a clean cloth. Voila! 

One last word of advice: don’t clean your jewelry over a bathroom or kitchen sink. Or, if you do, at the very least ensure the drain is plugged. Otherwise, we’ll spare you an “I told you so” if an earring falls down the drain.
