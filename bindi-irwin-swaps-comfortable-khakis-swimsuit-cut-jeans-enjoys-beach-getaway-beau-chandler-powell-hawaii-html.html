Bindi Irwin enjoys beach getaway with Chandler Powell in Hawaii

 Posted on February 18, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  801 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

She's been back in Queensland working at Australia Zoo following her successful stint on the American version of Dancing With The Stars.

And this month Bindi Irwin, the 17-year-old daughter of the late Crocodile Hunter Steve, took time to unwind during a family trip in Hawaii and was seen splashing around in the sea.

The effervescent Wildlife Warrior larked around with her American professional wakeboarder beau Chandler Powell, 19, as they tossed a tennis ball around while her brother Robert, 12, surfed.
