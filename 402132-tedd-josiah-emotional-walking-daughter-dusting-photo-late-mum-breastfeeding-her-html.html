Raising children singlehandedly without their mother or father around is one of the toughest journeys one cane go through.

While the same comes with a lot of challenges, it also comes with its own fair share of joy and emotions.

This, was recently exhibited in the life of revered music producer Tedd Josiah who has been raising his daughter alone ever since the death of his wife, Reginah Katar.

Tedd was recently moved to an emotional state after walking in on his daughter dusting photos of her late mum.

Upon reaching the room where his daughter was, he was thrown into disbelief by the sight of her daughter doing the aforementioned.

Judging by his post, it is clear the moment reminded him of his late wife as well as impressed him owing to his daughters touching move.

The post sparked emotions online with many of his followers expressing how moved they were by the young girls emotional move.

It should be noted that Tedd lost his wife on Saturday, September 30, in 2017.

According to earlier reports filed by TUKO.co.ke, Tedd's wofe Reginah died after a short illness.

Informing his worldwide friends, fans and other family members about the sudden demise of his wife, Tedd Josiah took to social media and appreciated the moments he was lucky to share with his love in a long post.

The former Vipi Producer could not send off his dear wife any better than thanking God even more for bringing them together.

The late Reginah left behind Tedd Josiah and their daughter Jameela alias Jay Jay who was only three months old then.
