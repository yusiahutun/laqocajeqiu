J-Hope of BTS’ sister, Jung Ji-woo

While Jung Ji-woo may have her little brother, J-Hope of BTS, to thank for her initial rise to fame, she has since become a star in her own right thanks to her good looks and fashionable style. She even signed with Cube Entertainment earlier this year as a YouTube vlogger and fashion influencer.

Her personal Instagram page, where she frequently posts photos of her travels and her outfits, has already amassed 9.3 million followers at the time of writing.

Jisoo of Blackpink’s sister, Kim Ji-yoon

While fans have praised Jisoo for her beauty long before her debut with supergroup Blackpink, many were surprised to discover that she isn’t the only stunner in her family. Jisoo’s older sister, Kim Ji-yoon, first became popular when she appeared on the childcare variety programme, Trio’s Childcare Challenge.

Fans were surprised to learn that Kim already has two children, considering she doesn’t look much older than her younger sister.

Rosé of Blackpink’s sister, Alice Park

But Jisoo is not the only Blackpink member with attractive siblings. Rosé’s older sister, Alice Park, also gained attention from Blinks – or Blackpink fans – for her stunning looks. Some fans have even pointed out that she looks like Rosé’s bandmate Jisoo from certain angles.

Minho of Shinee’s brother, Choi Min-seok

While Minho of Shinee has long been regarded as one of the best looking among second generation K-pop idols, it seems that his brother Choi Min-seok could pass off as an idol too.

Choi recently appeared on the sports entertainment programme Golf King, which then made him trend among Shinee fans in online forums. Fans discovered that he is not only good looking, but also intelligent too, as he’s a graduate of Seoul National University, the top university in South Korea.

Chanyeol of Exo’s sister, Park Yoo-ra

As a member of one of K-pop’s biggest boy bands, Exo’s Chanyeol always appears on our screens, but he isn’t the only one in his family with a face that’s great for television. His older sister, Park Yoo-ra, was a news anchor for many top broadcasting stations until her decision to switch careers in 2019.

While she’s no longer a regular on television, she is still a popular influencer, with 1.3 million fans on Instagram. Here, fans frequently comment on her beauty and her resemblance to her younger brother.
