Billionaire Taylor Swift dazzles in female-owned jewelry brand we can all afford

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  2 minutes  |  316 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Taylor Swift has stepped out in some show-stopping outfits over the past few months. To complete her chic looks, the Shake It Off singer has opted for some eye-catching jewels from a female-owned brand, Mazin Jewels.

Taylor has been showing off the jewelry brand while spending time with her friends, debuting her new romance with Travis Kelce, and strutting her stuff through New York. The Bejeweled singer is no stranger to accessorizing her fits and rounding off her image with a staple red lip.

Taylor Swift wears Mazin with Travis

Taylor Swift and her boyfriend were papped leaving Saturday Night Live back in October.

As the two held hands and made their exit, fans couldn’t help but notice her chunky gold chain.

Despite being a billionaire, Taylor opts for an understated necklace which has a price tag of $70.

The chain link necklace comes in colors gold and silver and is made from 18K gold-plated stainless steel.

Tayor wears rings from the brand

Swifties are going to be adding pieces from Mazin to their Christmas lists like there’s no tomorrow now that Taylor is wearing the brand on the regular.

The singer was spotted linking arms with Selena Gomez in November and two rings from the outlet can be seen on her left hand.

Taylor clutches a black handbag wearing two rings. She dons a Pave Snake Ring and an Open Rivet Ring which come in at around $50 each.

Singer stuns in New York

Walking through the Big Apple in a brown croc skirt, black top, and brown blazer, Taylor finished off her looks with another accessory from Mazin Jewels.

This time, she opted for the Toggle Clasp Necklace which is made from 18K gold plating and Cubic Zirconia stones.

The necklace has a price tag of around $70. It’s currently so popular that there could be a two-week wait for shipping.
