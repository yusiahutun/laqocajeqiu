Michigan girl laughs and cries hysterically after having four-wisdom teeth removed

 Posted on March 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  391 words  |  Martina Birk

Most people dread a trip to the dentist's chair, but this teenage girl seemed to be having the time of her life after a four-tooth extraction. 

A mum from Livonia, Michigan, recorded the hilarious aftermath of her daughter's trip for a wisdom tooth removal.

Still under strong anesthetic, the video shows the girl completely amazed at the sensation of losing four teeth.

The girl's mum then tells her to simply 'bite down on the gauze' that the doctors had given her to help with the numbed pain of her operation.

But the only reaction the girl can give is to stare blankly at her, as if she just explained the meaning of life, before starting to scream with laughter.

The surreal moment escalates when the girl checks her reflection in the sun shade mirror, howling uncontrollably at herself. 

She tries hard to hold a conversation, but because of the cotton lumps in her everything jumbles together.

As the laughter gets too much for the girl she can be vaguely heard saying 'this is too funny!' because continuing to giggle. 

Her mother tries to get her to hold it together as she prepares to drive off but the girl lashes out, totally unaware that her mother is attempting to drive.

Posting the video, the girl's mother said: 'My daughter had four wisdom teeth removed on Feb 22nd. This was how she behaved in the car afterward. 

'She fell asleep shortly after.'

Checking herself in the car mirror, the girl screams with laughter until it all gets too much and she starts crying over the numb pain

Wisdom tooth removal is a routine operation that takes about 45 minutes or more depending on how many teeth you have taken out.

In the US doctors usually give patients a choice of local anesthetic which is injected into your gums or an IV sedation into your arm which will help you sleep through.

You might be given laughing gas or nitrous oxide on top of this, which usually takes hours to wear off.
