He was said to be devastated when his partner of 19 years, Maia Norman, left him for another man.

But Damien Hirst, the world's richest artist, has proved he has well and truly put his woes behind him by stepping out with a stunning brunette 25 years his junior.

Pictured together for the first time last week, the 47-year-old was pictured enjoying a romantic stroll through London's Mayfair district with new girlfriend Roxie Nafousi following an intimate dinner at Scott's restaurant.

New romance: Damien and Roxie couldn't keep the contented smiles off their faces as they took a stroll through Mayfair together

The new couple made every effort to avoid being snapped together as they made their way into the upmarket eatery separately.
