About

Phoebe Bridgers is a 29 year old American Singer. Born Phoebe Lucille Bridgers on 17th August, 1994 in Pasadena, California, USA, she is famous for having released multiple tracks, albums, and EPs including Stranger in the Alps, Punisher, Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas, 7 O’Clock News/Silent Night (featuring Fiona Apple and Matt Berninger), Killer, Motion Sickness, Funeral, Kyoto, Garden Song, and Christmas Song, and for being part of the musical groups Boygenius (with Julien Baker and Lucy Dacus) and Better Oblivion Community Center (with Conor Oberst). Her zodiac sign is Leo.
