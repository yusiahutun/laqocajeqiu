Some of the most stylish male celebrities, from Harry Styles to Jake Gyllenhaal and Ryan Gosling all wear a chain from time to time and you’ll no doubt have spotted this year’s male Love Island contestants wearing one while they're grafting.

Roll back to 2020 and chains for men became something of a phenomenon, thanks to the TV adaptation of Sally Rooney’s Normal People - fans really couldn't get enough of one key part of the show: Connell’s chain.

The character's chain necklace, a subtle silver curb chain, sparked a major men's style trend that saw sales of chains for men go off the chart – and it’s still a major trend that you’ll see on men through the seasons. Plus, it makes a great Valentine's Day gift for him.

The good news is there are lots of styles for men, and these are the very best chains to shop.
