Sex museums around the world with kinky history, toys and real penis displays

There are plenty of museums in the world from natural history to ancient artefacts.

Many of the archives found in the UK are free, or cheap to enter and they provide hours or entertainment and education when on a day out.

Those who are keen history buffs may have already discovered some of the more unusual museums out there.

But, the world of niche and bizarre museums doesn’t stop there because it turns out there are a huge number of archives dedicated to the more erotic side of human nature.

Based in Bethnal Green the Vagina Museum had vagina themed exhibitions and welcomed over 40,000 people ready to learn about gynaecological anatomy since it opened 10 months ago.

It’s described as "the worlds first bricks and mortar museum dedicated to the gynaecological anatomy".

Exhibitions included Periods: A Brief History and From A to V.

Daily Star reporter Layla Nicholson travelled to the cheeky museum earlier this week – check out her review.

While the Vagina Museum was forced to vacate its current property this week the team behind it say they’re looking for a more permanent home to share the wonders of the V in future.

vaginamuseum.co.uk

Sex Museum, Amsterdam

Anyone heading the the Netherlands can shock and awe their pals with a visit to this famous museum.

You must be over 16 to enter, but its pinned as being an “educational and funny” look at sex through the ages.

As the oldest museum of its kind the 'Venustempel' in Amsterdam contains a large collection of erotic images, paintings, visual material, objects and more all to do with what we get up to in the bedroom.

Items on show include everything from vintage pornography to lingerie and sex toys.

sexmuseumamsterdam.nl

Leather Archives & Museum, Chicago

This museum was founded in 1991 and conserves the history of all things leather, kink, fetish and BDSM.

Depending on the day admission is either free or $10, but you have to be 18 or older to enter.

The museum features also throws plenty of events including “meditation for kinky peeps” and human “puppy and handler” contests.

leatherarchives.org

Sex Machines Museum, Prague

Zoning in on sex technology over the years this museum looks at all the machines and sex toys we humans have concocted.

Whether that’s erotic cinema clips, electric dildos or even chastity belts it’s all on show.

There are three floors with over 350 kinky items displayed.

Since its opening there have been plenty of adults giggling their way through the sex machine museums doors.

Once again you must be 18 or older to enter.

sexmachinesmuseum.com

The Icelandic Phallalogical Museum

It’s not just the vagina that gets its own exhibitions as the penis is celebrated in all its glory in Iceland.

Based in Reykjavik, this archive is dedicated to studying and showing off all things phallic.

That includes actual animal penises…

Plus, there’s even something to eat and drink at the phallic themed on-site bistro and cafe.

Pick up gifts for all your pals at the penis-bedecked gift shop too.
