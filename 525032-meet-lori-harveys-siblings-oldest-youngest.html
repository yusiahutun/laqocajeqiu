Lori Harvey’s siblings

Brandi Harvey

Brandi Harvey is the eldest in the family. She was born on August 20, 1982, making her 41 years old in 2023. Brandi is from Steve Harvey’s marriage to Marcia Harvey and is not biologically related to Lori Harvey.

She is an entrepreneur, motivational speaker, lifestyle coach, author and supporter of the vegan lifestyle. Brandi is also the founder of Beyond Her.

On top of these, Brandi has participated in an amateur bodybuilding competition where she won second place. She got the accolade because of her commitment to fitness, healthy eating, and wellness.

And that is why Brandi found Beyond Her. Its mission is “to bring active well to women of colour in collaborative and transformative ways to elevate the mind, body and spirit”.

Brandi is a recipient of the White House Drum Major of Service Award, issued by President Barrack Obama. She has also been on the stage at Harvard Business School, Princeton and NYU.

Karli Harvey

Karli Harvey is Brandi Harvey’s twin sister, which means she is also 41 years old in 2023. She is also from Steve Harvey's marriage to Marcia Harvey. Karli is a hairstylist and entrepreneur, magazine editor, and host, among many other things. Karli is based in Atlanta, Georgia.

Karli is a mother of two. She uses her time to mentor young women who are interested in fashion but have a tight budget. Karli often appears on YouTube on Steve Harvey’s TV show, sometimes with her husband, Benjamin Raymond.

Morgan Harvey

Morgan Harvey is the third born in Steve Harvey’s family. She was born on June 1, 1987, making her 36 years old as of 2023. Morgan Harvey is a professional cook, author, and businesswoman. She graduated from the Culinary Institute of America.

Jason Harvey

Jason Harvey is the first boy among Lori Harvey’s siblings. He was born in 1991 and is 32 years old in 2023. Jason is a fashion designer and is famous for the Worldly fashion label. He has been interested in fashion since childhood and has immense experience in the trade.

Wynton Harvey

Wynton Harvey is the youngest among Lori Harvey’s siblings. He was born on July 18, 1997, making him 26 years old as of 2023. He went to the Savannah College of Art and Design in his hometown of Atlanta, Georgia.

Wynton is close to his sister, Lori Harvey, appearing in a video titled “Wynton & Lori pack for college, posted on their father’s YouTube channel in 2015. The video has over 2 million views.
