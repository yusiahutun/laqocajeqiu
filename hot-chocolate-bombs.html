You can’t go wrong with chocolate—it’s a year-long vibe, whether you’re celebrating Valentine’s Day, an anniversary, or a birthday. Especially during the winter, it’s hard not to want to snuggle up on the couch and scarf down a bunch of chocolate. Last year, it seemed like hot cocoa bombs took over the world of sweets, and we’re here to say that this delicious trend is back, and tastier than ever. 

If you haven’t heard of them yet, here’s a quick debrief: Hot chocolate bombs are spheres of chocolate that are filled with hot cocoa mix and sometimes even marshmallows and sprinkles (YUM). They quite literally burst with flavor—and rich, indulgent flavor at that. 

Related Stories

These goodies are ideal for any sweet tooth out there who also loves an aesthetically pleasing and extremely Instagrammable foodie moment. They’re great gifts, too since the majority come in individually packaged boxes, but also in cute gift sets. Why keep buying the same old tins of chocolates for your family, friends and coworkers, when you can just as easily give them something even better? 

At this point, your mouth might already be watering, so we’ll spare you the extra chit-chat. Below, find 10 of the most festive hot chocolate bombs.
