The actress' tresses were seemingly dyed varying shades of pink, with a rich colour beginning at the roots and becoming faded towards the ends.

Pre-pink: To highlight the contrast of her new look, Chloe posted a picture in the same ensemble with her blonde locks against the same green hedges and more neutral make-up 

Sporting an unbuttoned flannelette T-shirt, the aspiring singer's perky assets were in full view in her low-cut black singlet top seemingly layered over a lacey bra.   

To highlight the contrast of her new look, Chloe posted a picture earlier in the same ensemble with her blonde locks against the same green hedges and more neutral make-up. 

In the busty beauty's newest image her make-up seems more intense with plenty more mascara and eyeliner in addition to contouring along her cheeks and a light orange shade of lipstick.

The aspiring singer’s dramatic transformation over the years has prompted many on social media to claim the singer has gone back to fillers and Botox.

Chloe has previously explained that while she did dabble with fillers in the past, her recently plumped pout is in fact due to a do-it-at-home product that uses a suction technique to help the lips swell up.

'Used my @fullipsenhancer today right before a shoot! Lovin my pout!' Chloe captioned a snap of herself staring into the camera last year.
