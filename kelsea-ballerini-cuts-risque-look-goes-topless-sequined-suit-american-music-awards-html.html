Kelsea Ballerini is gearing up to release her third studio album.

But first the artist was attending the American Music Awards on Sunday, where she was nominated for Favorite Female Artist – Country.

The singer cut a risque look as she went topless in a sequined suit on the red carpet for the awards show. 

Risque reveal: Kelsea Ballerini cut a risque look as she went topless in a sequined suit Sunday at the American Music Awards in Los Angeles

The 26-year-old donned a red and silver sequined suit jacket, which featured black tuxedo lapels.

She wore the sparkling jacket open, revealing her taut tummy and a glimpse at her décolletage.

Ballerini took to the stage at the end of the awards show to announce a performance by Shania Twain, whom she's cited as her greatest influence in the past.

In addition to her AMA nomination, Ballerini received nominations this year at the Grammy Awards, CMA Awards, CMT Awards and Teen Choice Awards.

In September, she released Homecoming Queen?, the lead single from her upcoming as yet untitled third studio album.

The song debuted at number 22 on the Country Airplay chart, making it her highest-charting debut yet. 

It was followed by the promotional single Club in November, which she recently performed at the People's Choice Awards.
