Where does Dave Chappelle live?

One might assume a big star like Dave lives in either New York City or Los Angeles. However, as it turns out, he lives in Yellow Springs, Ohio. Chappelle purchased the modest home in 2015. It is in the same area where he spent summers as a child with his father.

Dave Chapelle's house is a 39-acre farm with a private gated entrance. The house is approximately 3,154 square feet in size. It comes with three bedrooms and three bathrooms, perfectly cosy for his family of five. Current estimates place the home's value at $277,330.

Apart from Dave Chappelle's farm in Yellow Springs, he owns houses in Xenia, Ohio. Unfortunately, not many details about these houses are in the limelight. However, in 1993, he purchased a 4-bed, 2-bath, 2,770 sq ft home (62+ acre property total) for $300K. In addition, he owns at least two other homes in the area.
