Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Pregnant Nicoles Paternity Mystery DNA Test to Prove Whether Eric

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  407 words  |  Martina Birk

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers tease that Nicole Walker (Arianne Zucker) will face a total curveball when she’s rushed to the hospital in severe pain. Nicole’s ER visit will lead to a positive pregnancy test, so that’ll leave her concerned about what the future holds.

Of course, Nicole will face mixed emotions since part of her will love the idea of having a baby, but another part of her will fear this is only going to end in disappointment.

Since Nicole was told she’d never be able to carry a child to term, she’ll worry this pregnancy could bring a terrible tragedy.

Nicole may struggle to let herself dream of a future where she actually gives birth to a healthy and happy baby.

Although Nicole is a mom to Holly Jonas (Elin Alexander), Days fans will recall that Holly came into the world via surrogate.

Chloe Lane (Nadia Bjorlin) gave birth to Holly for Nicole, who assumed that was the only way she’d ever have a chance at motherhood.

That’s why Nicole will be taken back to learn she’s facing this high-risk pregnancy out of the blue.

Days spoilers say this could be Nicole’s shot at a miracle baby, but that can only happen if the child survives.

However, there’ll be another problem to contend with since Nicole won’t be sure who the father is. EJ DiMera (Dan Feuerriegel) will obviously be an option, but so will Eric Brady (Greg Vaughan) since Nicole hooked up with him during the drugged biscuit mayhem.

Days spoilers say Nicole will face a paternity mystery and will need a DNA test at some point.

That could bring some mixed emotions as well – not to mention there’s always a chance someone will tamper with the results!

Nicole’s pregnancy will leave her wondering who the daddy is, but we’d bet big bucks that Eric will be revealed as the true father in the end.

This “Ericole” baby could eventually become the catalyst for a reunion between Nicole and Eric down the road.

Regardless, DOOL viewers can expect Nicole’s life to get turned upside down in the coming months, so this should be an exciting storyline.

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Nicole’s baby news will cause chaos, so stay tuned for updates on the tricky path ahead.
