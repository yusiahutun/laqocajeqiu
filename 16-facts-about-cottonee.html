Cottonee’s Appearance

Cottonee is a small, white Pokemon that resembles a fluffy cotton ball. It has large, round blue eyes and a pair of small green leaf-like wings.

Grass and Fairy Type

Cottonee is a dual-type Pokemon, belonging to the Grass and Fairy types. This unique combination gives it a variety of strengths and weaknesses in battles.

Evolution into Whimsicott

Cottonee can evolve into Whimsicott when exposed to a Sun Stone. Whimsicott retains Cottonee’s fluffiness but gains a mischievous charm.

Natural Pranksters
