Lucky Philip Dube, famously known as Lucky Dube, was born on August 3, 1964, and died on October 18, 2007, at the age of 43 years. He was a famous South African reggae musician and Rastafarian. Over the course of his 25-year career, he recorded 22 albums in different languages, including Zulu, English, and Afrikaans.
