Patrick Fabian Welcomes Daughter Delilah Grace

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  172 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The Newsroom is buzzing: Patrick Fabian is a dad again!

The actor and his wife, comedienne and writer Mandy Steckelberg, welcomed their second daughter, Delilah Grace, on Saturday, July 28 in Los Angeles, his rep confirms to PEOPLE.

Born at home, the baby girl weighed 8 lbs., 12 oz. and measured 18 inches long. “Two is definitely more than one. Couldn’t be happier!” Fabian says.

Already dad to daughter Abbey Ray, 22 months, the proud papa jokes that he is surrounded by lovely ladies.

“Now I live in the House of Estrogen; a beautiful wife, two beautiful daughters, even my two dogs are girls,” Fabian, 47, tells PEOPLE exclusively. “I don’t think anyone’s gonna ever listen to me again!”

According to Fabian, who will next appear in Atlas Shrugged, his friends find his situation rather funny! “All my friends said, ‘God must have a sense of humor’ when they found out I was having another daughter,” he shares. “What exactly do they mean?”

— Anya Leon with reporting by Jennifer Garcia
