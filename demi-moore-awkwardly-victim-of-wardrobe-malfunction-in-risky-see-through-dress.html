Demi Moore awkwardly victim of wardrobe malfunction in risky see-through dress

 Posted on June 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  305 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Demi Moore has been bringing all the glam to Paris Fashion Week, but even stars slip up sometimes, which is exactly what the 60-year-old did in an extremely awkward wardrobe malfunction.

You can’t go wrong with an all-black outfit, and although the Hollywood star looked drop-dead gorgeous in her see-through frock, unfortunately, something did go slightly left.

Demi Moore’s awkward wardrobe malfunction

As the movie star attended Saint Laurent’s Womenswear Spring/Summer 2024 show, of course, she stood outside to pose for some snaps in her stunning black sheer gown.

Although the Ghost actress prepared with matching black underwear underneath, whilst posing for snaps, she lifted her arm up and accidentally flashed her breasts to the paps.

However, in true Demi style, she didn’t seem phased by the encounter and carried on posing with her sleeky straight black locks. She also added a pair of chic sunglasses to finish off the look, paired with some pointy stilettos.

The outing comes after Bruce Willis’ wife gave a heartbreaking update on his dementia diagnosis. Demi and Bruce were married for 11 years and share three daughters together.

Demi’s pooch steals the show

Although the actress has been serving looks at Paris and Milan fashion week, it looks like her dog, Pilaf has been the star of the show.

As she rocked the Versace Spring 2024 show in Milan in a showstopping pair of sparkly gold pants, all eyes were on Chihuahua, Pilaf.

The pooch has been going on a Fashion Week tour this summer, stopping off in Stockholm, Milan and most recently, joining his owner in Paris.

Pilaf is also a star on Instagram, as mom is forever showing the pooch off over on the gram. Most recently, Pilaf was seen posting on a ‘night out’ with the one and only, Donatella Versace.
