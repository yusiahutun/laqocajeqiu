Gary Dale Williams Jr. | Obituaries

 Posted on October 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  188 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Gary Dale Williams Jr., 26, of Waynesboro died Sunday, Oct. 21, 2018 in Waynesboro. He was born Wednesday, April 8, 1992 in Laurel.

Visitation will be 5-9 p.m. Thursday at the Liberty Baptist Church in Stringer. Funeral services will be at 11 a.m. Friday at Liberty Baptist Church and the burial will follow in Liberty Baptist Church Cemetery. Brother Alan McCord, Brother Tony Johnson and Brother William Lightsey will officiate. 

Mr. Williams was preceded in death by grandfather Bob Williams; great-grandmother Annie Mae Williams; and mother-in-law Patricia Smith.

Survivors include wife Savannah Williams; father Gary Williams Sr.; mother Teresa Jones; daughters McKennzie Williams, Madison Williams and Isabella Newell; son Mason Smith; brother Matthew Williams (Malerie); grandmothers Patricia Williams and Shirley Ainsworth; grandparents J.C. Jones (Linda); stepmother Rose Williams; stepfather Jonathon Dehaan; father-in-law Ricky Smith (Sherri); nieces Ainslie Williams and Olivia Williams; nephews Aidan Williams and Jayce Brady; aunts Beverly Lightsey (William), Christie Knotts (Joshua), Jennifer Rowland (Frankie), Kim Gatlin (Brian), Tina Delk (Hogey) and Nicole Ainsworth; uncles Chris Holifield (Carmen) and Charles Holifield.

Pallbearers will be family and friends.

An online guestbook may be signed at www.colonialchapelfuneralhomelaurel.com.
