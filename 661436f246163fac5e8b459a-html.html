Morgan Wallen video emerges from just moments before he allegedly threw chair off rooftop bar in Nas

 Posted on May 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  346 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Avideo emerged on TikTok from a customer at Eric Church's new honky tonk in Nashville, showing Morgan Wallen on his way to the rooftop bar. Just moments after the exchange, Wallen was arrested for throwing a chair off of the roof, which almost hit a Nashville police officer.

The country music star was charged with three counts of reckless endangerment and one count of disorderly conduct, for which he could face up to 15 years in jail if convicted. His bond was set at $15,250 and he was released from Davidson County Jail around 3:30am.

Morgan Wallen's history of legal trouble

This isn't the first time Wallen has had a run-in with the law in Nashville. In May 2020, he was arrested for public intoxication and disorderly conduct outside another honky-tonk on Lower Broadway.

Despite his legal troubles, Wallen has continued to rise in the country music scene, winning multiple Billboard Music Awards in 2023 and embarking on a nationwide tour.

Wallen's upcoming court appearance on May 3 coincides with a scheduled concert at Nissan Stadium in Nashville, raising questions about the future of his performances amidst his legal woes.

A fan met Morgan Wallen right before the incident

His alleged drunken outburst was a shock to the fan who posted the video from earlier in the night, when he was so friendly they couldn't believe their eyes. Here's what the fan wrote on TikTok about the encounter:

"So we are casually strolling Broadway and decide to go to Jason Aldeans restaurant. On the way to the roof area.. I spotted a familiar face. But this can't be happening.. right? Wrong. We somehow were the only people to notice MORGAN WALLEN in the back corner eating with a group. We patiently waited a bit away till he was leaving," the caption said.

"The video explains what happened next.. Morgan was so incredibly kind and genuine. I was so nervous and didn't want to bug him that I didn't ask for a picture! But then HE offered to get one with me!! Listen, we were fans before, but when people end up meeting your ridiculously high expectations of them.. you can only like them more."
