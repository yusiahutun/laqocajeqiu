How do natural toothpastes and regular toothpastes differ?

While "regular" toothpastes can feature a host of chemically-based cleansing agents and preservatives, natural options are usually formulated with organic and plant-based ingredients that tend to be gentler overall on gums and teeth. And, if you're looking to swap out your go-to tube for a cleaner option, you can still find natural toothpastes featuring fluoride that are approved by the American Dental Association.

What are some of the top ingredients to look for in the best natural toothpastes?

Natural toothpastes can still include a range of ingredients no matter how streamlined their formulas are. While these rotation staples may vary, some common ingredients to look out for can include essential oils, hydrated silica, salt, baking soda, and other plant-based actives.
