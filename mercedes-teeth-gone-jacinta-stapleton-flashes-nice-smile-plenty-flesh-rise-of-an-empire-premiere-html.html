Australian actress Jacinta Stapleton left the prosthetic teeth at home on Monday night, strutting the red carpet at the 300: Rise of an Empire premiere in Melbourne in a super sexy, revealing outfit.

Most recently known for her role as convicted Australian drug smuggler Schapelle Corby’s sister Mercedes in Channel Nine’s telemovie Schapelle, Jacinta swapped the courtroom shirts for a more red carpet savvy ensemble.

The 34-year-old actress wore a plunging playsuit with green and purple floral prints.

Mercedes who? Jacinta looked far from her Schapelle character at the Rise of an Empire premiere as she stepped out in a sexy playsuit

The actress attended the movie's premiere, to support her older brother Sullivan who is the lead male of the fantasy action flick.

36-year-old Sullivan was not at the premiere, after injuring himself in Bangkok last week.

He was on a short filming break from US television series Strike Back when the accident occurred.

Jacinta accessorised her statement number with simple gold bracelets and a sleek black clutch.

Her blonde locks were styled in loose waves, sitting just above her shoulders, complementing the sophisticated outfit.

The former Neighbours and Stingers star made a return to the acting scene in February after a few years out of the spotlight.

Look at that smile: No Mercedes Corby style teeth were in sight as Jacinta hit the red carpet

Distracting: Mercedes' teeth stole the spotlight in the Schapelle telemovie

She landed a lead role in the much-hyped Schapelle TV series, playing the convicted drug smuggler's sister.

But it was the prosthetic teeth she wore to achieve Mercedes's infamous overbite that had everyone talking after the show.

Not only did #mercedesteeth appear as a trending topic, but a parody Twitter account named Mercedes Teeth was opened.

Despite the popularity of those pearly whites, Jacinta looked glowing and happy on the red carpet last night.

Keeping it cool: Actress Michala Banas rocks casual chic in jeans

Also on the red carpet was Upper Middle Bogan star Michala Banas.

The 35-year-old actress rocked a casual chic look, teaming a cream embroidered blouse with cool blue jeans.

Michala certainly got into the spirit of the film, playfully aiming a 300: Rise of an Empire weapon prop at a guest whilst laughing.
