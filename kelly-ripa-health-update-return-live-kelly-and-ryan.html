The ABC News host spent most of her days at home

    Kelly Ripa returned Monday morning for the latest installment of , and finally revealed why she'd been absent for the past few episodes.

    The show mixed in previously recorded and live-taped segments over the past week, with the show's DJ, Deja Vu, stepping in beside Ryan Seacrest.
