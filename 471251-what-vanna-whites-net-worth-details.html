Lawsuits

Vanna has filed a few lawsuits, which she has won and lost. The lawsuits include:

Playboy lawsuit

In 1987, she appeared in Playboy magazine even though the photos were taken in 1982, a few months before she started working on Wheel of Fortune. The photographer sold her pictures five years later to Hugh Hefner and Playboy.

After the photos were published, she filed a $5.2 million lawsuit against Playboy. She claimed that the photos were not meant for mass publication thus, doing so was tarnishing her image. Playboy argued that Vanna was aware the photos were meant to be published as per their agreement. Eventually, the lawsuit was dropped.

Sony lawsuit

In 1993, she sued Samsung Electronics corporation after airing a commercial featuring a smiling robot that turned letters on a game show. She claimed that the company used her likeness without her permission. She won the lawsuit and was awarded $403,000 in damages.
